# SMG Side Brands - Business Model & Alignment Report
**Date:** February 28, 2026 | **Brands Analyzed:** 21 | **Critical Issues Found:** 47

---

## Executive Summary

A deep-dive audit into every brand's marketing docs reveals a **systemic alignment problem**: nearly every brand has contradictions between its Brand Foundation, Brand Info, Affiliate Monetization Plan, and Complete Brand Template documents. The most common pattern is the Complete Brand Template projecting 5-15x higher revenue than the Affiliate Monetization Plan, with different audience definitions, different taglines, and sometimes describing fundamentally different businesses.

### Portfolio-Wide Issues
1. **Revenue projection conflicts** in 18 of 19 documented brands (avg 3-7x spread)
2. **Tagline inconsistencies** in 14 of 19 brands
3. **Audience definition conflicts** in 15 of 19 brands
4. **Revenue split percentages differ** between docs in 12 of 19 brands
5. **Complete Brand Template vs. Affiliate Plan describe different businesses** in 6 brands

### Alignment Tier Summary

| Tier | Brands |
|------|--------|
| WELL ALIGNED | AI Content Studio Pro, PAUSEWELL, Digital Nomad Gear |
| MODERATE ISSUES | Smart Pet Tech, Sustainable Beauty Box, Mindful Gaming Zone |
| SIGNIFICANT ISSUES | Brain Bender, Rappers Looking for Beats, AI Record Label, Now Hiring STL, The Work Out Six Pack Stop |
