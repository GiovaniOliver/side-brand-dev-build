# Top 5 90-Day Revenue Execution Plans
**Date:** March 30, 2026

## Purpose

This file turns the revenue audit into a build order for the five best near-term product brands:

1. `Azure Tide Wine CO`
2. `Digital Nomad Gear`
3. `PAUSEWELL`
4. `Sustainable Beauty Box`
5. `The Workout Six Pack Stop`

Each plan focuses on:

- the first offer that should go live
- the partner stack needed to support it
- the lead magnet and CTA flow
- the 90-day execution sequence
- what should explicitly wait until later

## Operating Rules For These 90-Day Plans

1. Do not add more blog volume until the first offer and lead magnet are live.
2. Do not start custom manufacturing until demand is validated.
3. Every brand needs one primary CTA, one lead magnet, one first offer, and one email sequence before expansion.
4. Blog posts should route readers toward email capture and product pages, not just general traffic.
5. AdSense stays secondary on all five brands during the first 90 days.

## Azure Tide Wine CO

### 90-Day Objective
Launch a premium zero-inventory wine commerce model with a waitlist, a first gifting or bundle offer, and a clear path into a founder wine club.

### First Offer
- `Founders Collection` curated wine bundle or premium gift set.

### Secondary Offers
- Founder wine club membership
- corporate gifting inquiry
- tasting-event or hosted experience waitlist

### Recommended Partner Stack
- Wine production and DTC fulfillment: `KDM Global Partners`
- Checkout compliance: `DRINKS ASSURE`
- scale compliance layer: `Sovos ShipCompliant`
- later 3PL/club scale: `Wineshipping.com` or `Cellars Wine Club Fulfillment`
- store layer: `Shopify`

### Lead Magnet
- `The Azure Tide Entertaining Guide`
  Wine-pairing, hosting, and gifting guide for premium dinners and social events.

### CTA Map
- Homepage CTA: join the founder list for early access to the first curated release.
- Blog CTA: download the entertaining guide and join the founder list.
- Product CTA: reserve the founder bundle.
- B2B CTA: request a corporate gifting consultation.

### Conversion Flow
Luxury blog or social traffic -> entertaining guide opt-in -> founder email sequence -> limited founder bundle or gift order -> wine club invitation -> reorder and gifting upsell.

### Weeks 1-4
- Lock the launch model as zero-inventory partner-led commerce.
- Choose the first release format: gifting bundle, dinner bundle, or founder collection.
- Confirm the initial partner stack and collect legal/compliance requirements.
- Build founder-list landing page, lead magnet, and waitlist sequence.
- Rewrite homepage and top blog CTAs around the founder list and gifting.

### Weeks 5-8
- Finalize sample product set and pricing with the fulfillment partner.
- Build product detail pages for the founder bundle and corporate gifting inquiry.
- Publish buyer-intent content:
  premium gifting, dinner hosting, wine for celebrations, and curated pairings.
- Launch founder-list traffic from blog and social channels.
- Start outreach for restaurants, venues, and luxury-collab partners.

### Weeks 9-12
- Open the first founder bundle launch.
- Add a post-purchase sequence that invites buyers into the wine club.
- Start a small corporate gifting sales process.
- Review early conversion data and decide whether the second offer is club-first or gifting-first.

### Do Not Build Yet
- custom inventory ownership
- a broad catalog with too many bottle options
- non-alcoholic line extensions before the first wine offer converts

### Success Check By Day 90
- founder list active
- first bundle live
- compliance stack defined
- first repeatable post-purchase email flow in place

## Digital Nomad Gear

### 90-Day Objective
Launch an affiliate-plus-dropship travel-gear model built around buyer-intent SEO, a gear checklist lead magnet, and a tight category set.

### First Offer
- curated travel gear recommendations tied to a lead magnet and affiliate conversion pages

### Secondary Offers
- digital gear guide or packing system
- email deal roundups
- later dropship accessory bundles

### Recommended Partner Stack
- primary dropship source: `Spocket`
- trend testing source: `CJ Dropshipping`
- automation layer: `AutoDS`
- store layer: `Shopify`
- later private label: `PLCM`

### Lead Magnet
- `The Digital Nomad Carry-On Gear Checklist`

### CTA Map
- Homepage CTA: get the carry-on checklist.
- Blog CTA: view the recommended gear stack for this travel problem.
- Category CTA: compare the top 3 options.
- Email CTA: shop this week's nomad gear picks.

### Conversion Flow
Comparison blog traffic -> checklist opt-in -> email sequence with curated gear stacks -> affiliate purchase or dropship order -> newsletter deal flow -> later branded gear test.

### Weeks 1-4
- Limit launch categories to three:
  carry-on tech, organization gear, and charging/power accessories.
- Build the checklist lead magnet and welcome sequence.
- Create comparison page templates and product review structure.
- Add only a small starter catalog with the strongest supplier reliability.
- Tighten homepage and category-page CTAs around email capture and comparisons.

### Weeks 5-8
- Publish commercial-intent articles and comparison pages.
- Add curated gear-stack landing pages for different traveler types.
- Launch an email rhythm:
  welcome, best gear picks, category roundup, and trust-builder content.
- Test which traffic converts better:
  affiliate pages or direct dropship pages.

### Weeks 9-12
- Double down on top-performing categories.
- Add simple bundle pages and newsletter deal campaigns.
- Remove weak SKUs with low click or conversion rates.
- Decide whether one product category is ready for a small private-label test.

### Do Not Build Yet
- full private-label travel gear line
- too many low-signal categories
- heavy social content expansion before the comparison engine is live

### Success Check By Day 90
- checklist lead magnet live
- 3 launch categories active
- comparison pages converting traffic
- first data set on affiliate vs dropship performance

## PAUSEWELL

### 90-Day Objective
Launch the digital foundation first, validate demand with a menopause offer ladder, and prepare supplements as a second-stage conversion rather than the opening move.

### First Offer
- `The Menopause Mastery Course`

### Secondary Offers
- `PAUSEWELL Method Membership`
- menopause starter supplement waitlist
- quarterly wellness box waitlist

### Recommended Partner Stack
- test supplement layer: `Supliful`
- scale manufacturer: `Vitalpax`
- later larger manufacturer: `Makers Nutrition`
- supplement and box fulfillment: `Jay Group`
- legal/compliance review: supplement compliance counsel
- store and subscription layer: `Shopify` plus membership stack

### Lead Magnet
- `Menopause Symptom Reset Guide` or menopause assessment quiz

### CTA Map
- Homepage CTA: take the menopause reset quiz.
- Blog CTA: get the symptom-reset guide.
- Course CTA: join the first cohort of Menopause Mastery.
- Membership CTA: continue support after the course.

### Conversion Flow
Wellness SEO or social education -> quiz or guide opt-in -> email nurture -> course enrollment -> membership upsell -> supplement starter offer -> quarterly box offer.

### Weeks 1-4
- Lock the digital-first offer ladder.
- Build the quiz or lead magnet and welcome email sequence.
- Package the course outline, outcomes, pricing, and sales page.
- Review all health claims and marketing language for compliance.
- Rewrite homepage and blog CTAs around quiz -> course, not supplements first.

### Weeks 5-8
- Launch course presale or first cohort enrollment.
- Open a small founding membership beta.
- Add content around symptom education, sleep, mood, and energy.
- Collect supplement interest data through a waitlist and survey.
- Build the supplement validation page without overcommitting to inventory.

### Weeks 9-12
- Run the first course cohort.
- Convert members into the ongoing subscription layer.
- Validate which supplement SKU should launch first.
- Start vendor outreach for the first supplement run and box roadmap.

### Do Not Build Yet
- full 4-SKU inventory purchase
- large quarterly box rollout before digital conversion data exists
- aggressive claims language on product or blog pages

### Success Check By Day 90
- quiz or guide live
- course offer live
- membership beta active
- supplement demand validated before inventory commitment

## Sustainable Beauty Box

### 90-Day Objective
Launch a curated eco-beauty subscription model with a quiz, founding-subscriber waitlist, and a small initial supplier set before any private-label expansion.

### First Offer
- founding subscriber beauty box

### Secondary Offers
- clean beauty educational email series
- curated standalone product recommendations
- future private-label hero item

### Recommended Partner Stack
- wholesale and box sourcing: `Faire`, `DR.HC Organics`, `Beauty by Earth`, `Orglamix`
- private label later: `DR.HC Organics YOURS`, `Blanka`, or `Pravada`
- fulfillment: `ShipMonk` or `ShipBots`
- eco packaging: `EcoEnclose` or `noissue`
- store/subscription stack: `Shopify` plus `Recharge`

### Lead Magnet
- `Clean Beauty Starter Guide` or skin-type quiz

### CTA Map
- Homepage CTA: take the clean beauty quiz and join the founding box list.
- Blog CTA: get the clean beauty starter guide.
- Box CTA: reserve the founding subscriber spot.
- Email CTA: choose your skin profile and join the first shipment wave.

### Conversion Flow
Beauty SEO and social content -> quiz or guide opt-in -> waitlist nurture -> founding box order -> recurring subscription -> later private-label cross-sell.

### Weeks 1-4
- Decide the initial box cadence and price point.
- Build the quiz, founding waitlist, and welcome sequence.
- Shortlist 6-8 supplier-ready products for the first box.
- Define the unboxing promise and sustainability proof points.
- Tighten homepage and blog CTAs toward the founding box list.

### Weeks 5-8
- Finalize initial supplier conversations and sample review.
- Create the founding box landing page and FAQ.
- Publish ingredient education and clean-beauty buyer-intent blog content.
- Set up the subscription and fulfillment workflow.
- Capture founding-subscriber demand before overbuilding inventory.

### Weeks 9-12
- Launch the founding subscriber offer.
- Gather feedback on product mix, packaging, and reorder intent.
- Identify the best candidate for a later private-label hero product.
- Build the first upsell layer:
  add-on products, gift options, or longer subscription terms.

### Do Not Build Yet
- a full private-label line
- too many supplier relationships at once
- a broad marketplace catalog before the box offer is converting

### Success Check By Day 90
- quiz and waitlist live
- first box product set chosen
- founding box launched
- clear signal on whether private-label should focus on skincare or cosmetics first

## The Workout Six Pack Stop

### 90-Day Objective
Launch a digital-first fitness funnel anchored by a challenge or program, then layer coaching and merch after the core program starts converting.

### First Offer
- `21-Day Six Pack Challenge` or equivalent low-friction starter program

### Secondary Offers
- full premium training program
- coaching
- branded apparel
- later supplements

### Recommended Partner Stack
- checkout and digital delivery: course and payment stack already in site flow
- apparel POD: `Apliiq` and `Printify`
- later equipment: `Keepufitness`
- later supplements: `Pure Private Label`

### Lead Magnet
- `7-Day Core Reset Plan`

### CTA Map
- Homepage CTA: start the 7-day core reset.
- Blog CTA: join the 21-day challenge.
- Program CTA: upgrade into the premium training system.
- Follow-up CTA: book coaching or buy branded apparel.

### Conversion Flow
Fitness search traffic -> lead magnet opt-in -> challenge offer -> premium program or coaching -> apparel upsell -> later supplement offer.

### Weeks 1-4
- Fix the core offer packaging and define one starter program.
- Build the lead magnet and challenge sales flow.
- Align homepage, blog posts, and category pages to challenge-first CTAs.
- Package the premium upsell that follows the starter offer.

### Weeks 5-8
- Launch the challenge and email sequence.
- Publish search-intent blog content around ab training, fat loss support, and home routines.
- Add a coaching application or booking path.
- Test which call-to-action converts best:
  challenge, premium plan, or coaching.

### Weeks 9-12
- Launch the premium program upsell.
- Add a simple merch test with one or two apparel SKUs.
- Keep supplement planning in research mode until the digital funnel proves conversion.
- Build repeat retention emails tied to training progress and upsells.

### Do Not Build Yet
- supplement inventory
- a broad merch catalog
- multiple unrelated fitness offers competing on the homepage

### Success Check By Day 90
- lead magnet live
- challenge funnel active
- premium upsell active
- coaching and merch added only after challenge data is visible

## Shared KPI Layer For All Five Brands

Track these every week:

1. unique visitors
2. lead magnet opt-in rate
3. first-offer conversion rate
4. average order value
5. email click-through rate
6. repeat purchase or second-offer conversion rate
7. top-performing traffic source
