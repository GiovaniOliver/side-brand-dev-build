## ROOT CAUSE OF CONTRADICTIONS (SYSTEMIC)

**Every brand that shows conflicting revenue numbers, audience definitions, or business models has the same underlying cause:**

The **Affiliate Monetization Plan** and **Complete Brand Template** were written independently with different assumptions and were never reconciled:

- **Affiliate Monetization Plan** = realistic bootstrapped launch plan. Year 1 targets: $60K–$400K. Affiliate/content-first business model.
- **Complete Brand Template** = aspirational platform-scale vision. Year 1 targets: $500K–$3.75M. Subscription/platform/membership model.

**These are Phase 1 vs Phase 3 targets — not contradictions — but they were written as if they were the same plan.**

### The Fix (applied to all brands below):
- **Phase 1 Revenue Target** = Affiliate Monetization Plan number (realistic launch goal)
- **Phase 2–3 Scale Vision** = Complete Brand Template number (aspirational, not for launch docs)
- **Canonical Audience** = the most specific, researched definition (usually Brand Foundation + Brand Info)
- **Canonical Tagline** = the one in Brand Foundation (override others)
