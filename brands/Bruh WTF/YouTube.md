1. **Hook** (0-5s): Best clip preview
2. **Intro** (5-10s): Quick branding
3. **Clips** (10s-10min): Fast-paced content
4. **Outro** (10-15s): Subscribe reminder
5. **End Screen** (15-20s): Next videos
