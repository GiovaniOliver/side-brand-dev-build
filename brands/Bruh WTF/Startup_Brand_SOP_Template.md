# Bruh WTF - Startup Brand SOP Template

## Standard Operating Procedures Overview

### SOP Mission
"To establish systematic, repeatable processes that ensure consistent quality, efficiency, and growth across all Bruh WTF operations while maintaining the brand's authentic humor and community focus."

### Primary SOP Goals
1. **Quality Consistency**: Maintain high standards across all content and interactions
2. **Operational Efficiency**: Streamline workflows for maximum productivity
3. **Scalability Planning**: Create processes that support rapid growth
4. **Team Coordination**: Ensure seamless collaboration and communication
5. **Brand Protection**: Safeguard brand reputation and community trust

## Daily Operations SOPs

### Content Creation Daily Workflow
**Morning Routine (7:00-9:00 AM)**:
1. **Trend Monitoring** (30 minutes):
   - Check TikTok trending sounds and hashtags
   - Review Instagram trending topics and formats
   - Analyze YouTube trending videos in comedy/entertainment
   - Monitor Twitter trending topics for humor opportunities
   - Document trending elements in content planning sheet

2. **Community Engagement Review** (45 minutes):
   - Respond to overnight comments across all platforms
   - Review user-generated content submissions
   - Check direct messages and community inquiries
   - Update community challenge participation tracking
   - Identify potential viral community content

3. **Content Planning** (45 minutes):
   - Review daily content calendar and scheduled posts
   - Adjust content based on trending topics discovered
   - Plan cross-platform content adaptation
   - Schedule filming and creation time blocks
   - Prepare props and setup for video creation

**Content Production Block (9:00 AM-2:00 PM)**:
1. **Video Filming Session** (2 hours):
   - Setup equipment and lighting according to checklist
   - Film 3-5 TikTok videos following trending formats
   - Record 1-2 Instagram Reels with platform optimization
   - Capture behind-the-scenes content for Stories
   - Film YouTube Shorts and longer-form content weekly

2. **Photo and Graphics Creation** (1 hour):
   - Create meme graphics using brand templates
   - Design Instagram carousel posts
   - Prepare thumbnail images for videos
   - Update website graphics and promotional materials
   - Maintain consistent brand visual identity

3. **Content Editing and Post-Production** (2 hours):
   - Edit videos using established brand style guide
   - Add captions, graphics, and brand elements
   - Optimize content for each platform's specifications
   - Create multiple versions for cross-platform use
   - Quality check for brand consistency and humor

**Afternoon Engagement (2:00-6:00 PM)**:
1. **Real-Time Posting and Engagement** (2 hours):
   - Post scheduled content across all platforms
   - Monitor initial engagement and respond to comments
   - Participate in trending conversations and challenges
   - Cross-promote content between platforms
   - Engage with community and other creators

2. **Analytics and Performance Review** (1 hour):
   - Track daily performance metrics across platforms
   - Identify top-performing content and engagement drivers
   - Document successful content formats and styles
   - Update performance tracking spreadsheets
   - Plan optimization strategies for underperforming content

3. **Content Planning for Next Day** (1 hour):
   - Review upcoming content calendar
   - Research trending topics and opportunities
   - Prepare props and materials for next day filming
   - Update content ideas list with community suggestions
   - Coordinate with any collaboration partners

### Community Management SOP
**Response Time Standards**:
- TikTok comments: Within 2 hours during peak times (6-10 PM EST)
- Instagram DMs and comments: Within 1 hour during business hours
- YouTube comments: Within 4 hours of posting
- Twitter mentions and replies: Within 30 minutes during active hours
- Email inquiries: Within 24 hours maximum

**Community Interaction Guidelines**:
1. **Brand Voice Consistency**:
   - Always maintain playful, positive, and inclusive tone
   - Use humor appropriately without offensive content
   - Respond with enthusiasm and genuine engagement
   - Include relevant emojis and brand catchphrases
   - Redirect negative comments to positive outcomes

2. **Engagement Strategies**:
   - Ask questions to encourage further conversation
   - Share user content with proper credit and permission
   - Create polls and interactive content for participation
   - Respond to comments with video replies when appropriate
   - Highlight community achievements and milestones

3. **Crisis Management Protocol**:
   - Escalate controversial or sensitive comments immediately
   - Never engage in arguments or negative discussions
   - Respond professionally to criticism with improvement focus
   - Document all incidents for pattern analysis
   - Consult team before responding to major issues

### Email Marketing Daily SOP
**Daily Newsletter Preparation** (5:00-7:00 PM):
1. **Content Selection and Curation** (45 minutes):
   - Review day's top-performing content across platforms
   - Select featured viral moment for newsletter
   - Curate community spotlight and user submissions
   - Prepare social media roundup and highlights
   - Write daily question and interactive element

2. **Newsletter Assembly and Design** (45 minutes):
   - Use established email template and brand design
   - Insert selected content with proper formatting
   - Add social media links and engagement calls-to-action
   - Include merchandise promotions and updates
   - Proofread for grammar, links, and brand consistency

3. **Quality Assurance and Sending** (30 minutes):
   - Send test email to team for review and approval
   - Check all links and images for proper display
   - Review mobile and desktop email formatting
   - Schedule send time for 8:00 AM EST next morning
   - Update email analytics tracking and attribution

## Quality Control and Brand Standards

### Content Quality Checklist
**Pre-Publication Review Process**:
1. **Brand Consistency Check**:
   ☐ Content aligns with brand voice and personality
   ☐ Visual elements match brand style guide
   ☐ Humor is inclusive and non-offensive
   ☐ Catchphrases and taglines used appropriately
   ☐ Brand colors and fonts used consistently

2. **Technical Quality Standards**:
   ☐ Video resolution meets platform requirements (1080p minimum)
   ☐ Audio quality clear and free of background noise
   ☐ Lighting adequate for clear visibility
   ☐ Text and graphics readable on mobile devices
   ☐ File formats optimized for each platform

3. **Engagement Optimization**:
   ☐ Hook within first 3 seconds for video content
   ☐ Call-to-action included and clearly stated
   ☐ Trending hashtags and sounds incorporated
   ☐ Content encourages comments and sharing
   ☐ Cross-platform promotion elements included

### Customer Service Standards
**Customer Interaction Guidelines**:
1. **Response Quality Standards**:
   - Always respond with positive and helpful tone
   - Provide specific solutions or next steps
   - Include relevant links or resources when helpful
   - Thank customers for their engagement and support
   - Follow up on unresolved issues within 24 hours

2. **Issue Resolution Process**:
   - Acknowledge customer concern immediately
   - Gather necessary information for resolution
   - Escalate complex issues to appropriate team member
   - Provide realistic timeline for resolution
   - Follow up with customer after issue resolution

3. **Customer Feedback Integration**:
   - Document all feedback in customer service database
   - Share positive feedback with team for motivation
   - Analyze negative feedback for improvement opportunities
   - Implement reasonable suggestions and improvements
   - Thank customers for valuable feedback and suggestions
