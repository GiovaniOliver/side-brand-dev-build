# Bruh WTF - Content Strategy & Calendar

## Content Strategy Overview

### Content Mission
"To create and curate the most shareable, surprising, and genuinely funny content that brings daily laughter to millions while building the internet's most engaged humor community."

### Strategic Content Objectives
1. **Viral Content Generation**: Create 10+ viral moments monthly (1M+ views)
2. **Community Engagement**: Maintain 15%+ engagement rate across platforms
3. **Content Volume**: Produce 150+ pieces of content monthly
4. **Revenue Attribution**: Generate $25K+ monthly through content monetization
5. **Brand Recognition**: Establish "Bruh WTF" as synonymous with quality humor

### Content Quality Standards

#### Technical Requirements
**Video Content**:
- Minimum 1080p resolution for all platforms
- Clear audio with noise reduction
- Consistent lighting and visual quality
- Platform-optimized aspect ratios
- Engaging thumbnails with brand consistency

**Image Content**:
- High-resolution graphics and photography
- Consistent brand colors and typography
- Mobile-optimized viewing experience
- Watermark or brand integration
- Platform-specific sizing and formatting

**Text Content**:
- Grammar and spelling accuracy
- Brand voice consistency
- Platform character limit optimization
- Hashtag research and optimization
- Engagement-driving calls to action

#### Creative Standards
**Humor Guidelines**:
- Inclusive and non-offensive content only
- Avoid controversial or divisive topics
- Maintain positive and uplifting tone
- Respect copyright and fair use laws
- Credit all sources and collaborations

**Brand Consistency**:
- Consistent voice and personality
- Regular catchphrase and tagline usage
- Visual brand element integration
- Community value alignment
- Professional presentation standards
