# Bruh WTF - Sales Funnel Strategy

## Sales Funnel Overview

### Funnel Mission
"To systematically convert humor-seeking audiences into engaged community members and paying customers through viral content experiences that drive authentic connections and sustainable revenue growth."

### Primary Funnel Goals
1. **Viral Traffic Generation**: 10M+ monthly impressions across all platforms
2. **Community Building**: 2M+ engaged followers within 12 months
3. **Email List Growth**: 100K+ newsletter subscribers
4. **Revenue Generation**: $25K+ monthly from multiple revenue streams
5. **Brand Loyalty Creation**: 70%+ customer retention and advocacy

## Complete Sales Funnel Architecture

### Stage 1: Awareness (Viral Content Discovery)
**Objective**: Capture attention through viral, shareable humor content

#### Traffic Generation Strategies
**Organic Viral Content** (70% of traffic):
- TikTok viral challenges and trending participation
- Instagram Reels with humor and surprise elements
- YouTube Shorts optimized for algorithm discovery
- Twitter viral tweets and trending topic participation
- Facebook shareable video content and memes

**Paid Social Amplification** (20% of traffic):
- TikTok ads promoting viral content
- Instagram promoted posts and story ads
- YouTube pre-roll ads on comedy content
- Facebook boosted posts with viral potential
- Cross-platform retargeting campaigns

**Organic Discovery** (10% of traffic):
- Search engine optimization for humor keywords
- Influencer mentions and collaborations
- Media coverage and press mentions
- Word-of-mouth and organic sharing
- Platform algorithm recommendation systems

#### Viral Content Strategy
**Hook Development**:
- First 3 seconds must capture attention
- Unexpected twists and surprise elements
- Relatable situations with humorous outcomes
- Pattern interrupts and attention-grabbing visuals
- Trending audio and sound integration

**Shareability Factors**:
- Content that triggers emotional response (laughter)
- Highly relatable to target audience experiences
- Easy to understand and immediately funny
- Encourages tagging friends and sharing
- Creates conversation and discussion
