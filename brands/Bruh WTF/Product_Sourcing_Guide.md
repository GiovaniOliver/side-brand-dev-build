# Bruh WTF - Product Sourcing Guide

## Print-on-Demand for Comedy/Meme Merch

### 1. Printify (PRIMARY - BEST MARGINS)
- **Website:** [printify.com](https://printify.com)
- **Products:** 1,300+ items - tees, stickers, mugs, phone cases, posters, tote bags
- **Pricing:** Bella+Canvas tees from ~$9.11, mugs ~$5, stickers ~$2
- **Target Margins:** 40%+ ($9 tee sells for $25+, $2 sticker sells for $5-8)
- **Plans:** Free or Premium $24.99/mo (20% off all products)

### 2. Redbubble (PASSIVE MARKETPLACE)
- **Website:** [redbubble.com](https://www.redbubble.com)
- **Model:** Upload designs, they handle everything
- **Margins:** 10-30% markup
- **Pros:** Built-in audience for humor/niche content, massive product variety
- **Note:** New 2025 fee structure ($0-$97/month based on earnings tier)

### 3. Merch by Amazon (PRIME TRAFFIC)
- **Website:** [merch.amazon.com](https://merch.amazon.com) (invite-only)
- **Royalties:** $2-$8 per shirt depending on price tier
- **Pros:** Massive organic traffic, Prime shipping boosts conversion
- **Cons:** Invite-only, strict content moderation on edgy humor

### Best Products for Comedy Brands
1. **Stickers** - lowest cost, highest impulse-buy rate
2. **T-shirts** - bread and butter
3. **Mugs** - gift-friendly, high margin
4. **Phone cases** - visual impact, impulse buys

### Recommended Strategy
- **Primary:** Printify + Shopify (owned brand, full margins)
- **Passive:** Redbubble (organic marketplace traffic for humor designs)
- **Traffic:** Apply for Merch by Amazon (Prime flywheel)
