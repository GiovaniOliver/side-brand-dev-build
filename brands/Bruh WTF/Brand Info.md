# Bruh WTF - Brand Info

## Overview
**Bruh WTF** is a humor and entertainment brand that creates and curates the most outrageous, funny, and surprising content from around the world, building a community united by shared laughter and unexpected moments.

## Brand Foundation

### Mission Statement
"To create a platform that brings humor, entertainment, and a sense of community to people by sharing the most outrageous, funny, and surprising content from around the world."

### Vision Statement
"To be the go-to destination for humor and entertainment, making every day a bit more enjoyable and connected through shared laughter and surprises."

### Primary Taglines
1. **"Expect the Unexpected"** (Primary)
2. "Laugh. Share. Repeat."
3. "Your Daily Dose of WTF"
4. "Where Humor Meets Surprise"
5. "Life's Funniest Moments"

## Core Values
1. **Humor**: Embrace and share laughter in every form
2. **Community**: Foster belonging through shared experiences
3. **Surprise**: Deliver unexpected and delightful content
4. **Inclusivity**: Welcome all voices and perspectives in fun
5. **Creativity**: Encourage original and innovative content
6. **Positivity**: Spread joy through humor
7. **Engagement**: Build vibrant community interactions

## Brand Persona & Voice
- **Tone**: Casual, playful, and engaging
- **Language**: Simple, relatable, and humorous
- **Personality**: Friendly, witty, and spontaneous

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Vibrant Blue (#007BFF)**: Trust and excitement
- **Electric Yellow (#FFEB3B)**: Energy and attention
- **Bold Red (#FF3D00)**: Urgency and surprise
- **Playful Green (#00C853)**: Fun and positivity
- **Neutral Gray (#9E9E9E)**: Modern balance

### Photography Style
- **Candid Shots**: Spontaneous, genuine reactions
- **Bright and Vivid**: High contrast, energetic vibes
- **Close-Ups**: Facial expressions and reactions
- **Diverse Subjects**: Inclusive humor representation
- **Playful Props**: Quirky elements for surprise

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: Ages 18-34
- **Interests**: Humor, memes, viral content, social media trends
- **Platforms**: Facebook, YouTube, Instagram, Reddit, TikTok, Twitter
- **Behavior**: Active social media users, content sharers, humor seekers

## Content Strategy

### Content Types
1. **Memes and Funny Images**: Trending and original humor
2. **Short Videos**: Surprising and humorous clips
3. **Interactive Posts**: Polls, quizzes, audience participation
4. **Contests and Challenges**: Weekly/monthly engagement
5. **Behind-the-Scenes**: Fun brand personality
6. **User-Generated Content**: Community submissions

### Posting Frequency
- **Facebook**: 3-4 posts per week
- **Instagram**: Daily posts and stories
- **Twitter**: 2-3 tweets per day
- **TikTok**: 3-4 videos per week
- **YouTube**: 1-2 videos per week
- **Reddit**: 2-3 posts per week

- **Social Media Growth**: Building engaged audience across platforms
