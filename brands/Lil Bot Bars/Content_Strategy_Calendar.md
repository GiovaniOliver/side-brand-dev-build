### Weekly Content Production Workflow

#### Content Planning and Development
**Sunday Planning Session (2 hours)**:
- Review previous week's performance and engagement metrics
- Plan upcoming week's content themes and specific topics
- Schedule collaboration sessions and guest appearances
- Prepare beat selections and musical content
- Update content calendar with current events and trends

**Monday-Tuesday Creation Block (4 hours daily)**:
- Film and record primary content for the week
- Conduct interviews and collaboration sessions
- Write and develop new musical content
- Capture behind-the-scenes and authentic moments
- Photograph and document visual content

**Wednesday-Thursday Post-Production (3 hours daily)**:
- Edit video content and audio recordings
- Create graphics, thumbnails, and visual elements
- Write captions, descriptions, and engagement copy
- Schedule content across all platforms
- Prepare backup content for unexpected situations

**Friday-Saturday Engagement Focus (2 hours daily)**:
- Respond to comments and community interactions
- Engage with other artists and industry professionals
- Participate in trending conversations and challenges
- Monitor performance metrics and adjust strategy
- Plan weekend live sessions and real-time content
