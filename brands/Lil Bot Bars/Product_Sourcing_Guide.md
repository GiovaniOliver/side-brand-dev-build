# Lil Bot Bars - Product Sourcing Guide

## Print-on-Demand for Artist Merch

### 1. Fourthwall (BEST FOR MUSIC ARTISTS)
- **Website:** [fourthwall.com](https://fourthwall.com)
- **No monthly fees**, keep 100% of physical product profits
- **Integrations:** YouTube Merch Shelf, Spotify, TikTok Shop, Discord
- **Also sells:** Digital products (beat packs, unreleased tracks) alongside physical merch

### 2. Printful (BEST QUALITY - ALL-OVER PRINTS)
- **Website:** [printful.com/band-merchandise](https://www.printful.com/band-merchandise)
- **Key Products:** All-over print hoodies (album art wraps entire garment), embroidered hats, wall tapestries, photo-realistic tees
- **Bulk Discounts:** Up to 55% off at 25+ units
- **Integrations:** Shopify, YouTube Merch Shelf, Spotify

### 3. Printify (BEST MARGINS FOR VOLUME)
- **Website:** [printify.com](https://printify.com)
- **Products:** T-shirts, hoodies, joggers, snapbacks, beanies, posters
- **Lower base costs** = better margins when a song/video goes viral

### 4. Awkward Styles (FAST ALL-OVER PRINTS)
- **Website:** [awkwardstyles.com](https://www.awkwardstyles.com)
- **Specializes in:** All-over print with fast turnaround
- **Integrations:** Shopify, WooCommerce

### Recommended Strategy
- **Storefront:** Fourthwall (Spotify + YouTube integration for artist)
- **Quality Items:** Printful for all-over print hoodies and embroidery
- **Volume/Basic:** Printify for standard tees and accessories
- **Special Drops:** Local screen printer for album releases/shows (authentic "merch table" feel)
