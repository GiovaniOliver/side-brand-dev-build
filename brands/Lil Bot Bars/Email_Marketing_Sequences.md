### A/B Testing Strategy

#### Subject Line Testing
**Testing Variables**:
- Personal vs. music-focused subject lines
- Emoji usage vs. text-only approaches
- Question format vs. statement format
- Artist name inclusion vs. content focus
- Urgency vs. curiosity-driven headlines

#### Content Testing
**Format Testing**:
- Video-first vs. audio-first content
- Personal story vs. music-focused emails
- Long-form vs. short-form content
- Educational vs. entertainment focus
- Artist voice vs. team communication

#### Send Time Optimization
**Testing Schedule**:
- Weekday vs. weekend sending
- Morning vs. evening delivery times
- Time zone considerations for global audience
- Holiday and special event timing
- Music release coordination
