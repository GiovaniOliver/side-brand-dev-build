## Target Audience Segments

### Primary: Puzzle Enthusiasts (Age 25-65)
- **Interests**: Brain games, crosswords, sudoku, logical thinking
- **Behavior**: Daily puzzle solvers, cognitive improvement seekers
- **Pain Points**: Lack of progressive difficulty, limited community interaction
- **Marketing Channels**: Facebook, puzzle forums, brain training apps

### Secondary: Students and Professionals
- **Interests**: Academic performance, productivity, mental sharpness
- **Behavior**: Study break activities, professional development
- **Pain Points**: Stress management, cognitive fatigue, focus improvement
- **Marketing Channels**: LinkedIn, educational platforms, productivity blogs

### Tertiary: Seniors and Caregivers
- **Interests**: Mental health, cognitive maintenance, social engagement
- **Behavior**: Health-conscious, community-oriented, family-focused
- **Pain Points**: Cognitive decline concerns, social isolation, technology barriers
- **Marketing Channels**: Facebook, health websites, senior communities
