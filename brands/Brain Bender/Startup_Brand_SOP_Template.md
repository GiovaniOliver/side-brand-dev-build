# Brain Bender - Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

## 1. Company Formation and Legal Setup

### Business Registration and Licensing
- Register Brain Bender as LLC in Missouri
- Obtain necessary business licenses for digital content platform
- Register trademark for "Brain Bender" brand name and logo
- Establish tax ID (EIN) for business operations

### Intellectual Property Management
- Trademark puzzle designs and unique game mechanics
- Copyright all original puzzle content and educational materials
- Implement content licensing agreements for user-generated puzzles
- Protect proprietary brain training algorithms and scoring systems

### Regulatory Compliance Tracking
- Ensure COPPA compliance for users under 13
- Implement GDPR compliance for international users
- Maintain accessibility standards (ADA compliance)
- Regular privacy policy and terms of service updates
