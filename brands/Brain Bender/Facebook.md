# Brain Bender - Facebook Strategy

## Platform Overview
Facebook serves as Brain Bender's community hub and educational resource center, focusing on building long-term relationships with puzzle enthusiasts, sharing in-depth brain training content, and creating engagement through groups and educational posts.

## Content Strategy

### Core Content Pillars

#### 1. Educational Brain Training (40% of content)
- **Cognitive Science Articles**: In-depth posts about brain health and training
- **Puzzle-Solving Techniques**: Detailed tutorials and strategy guides
- **Brain Health Tips**: Practical advice for mental fitness and wellness
- **Research Insights**: Latest findings in cognitive science and neurology
- **Memory Enhancement Guides**: Comprehensive memory improvement strategies

#### 2. Daily Puzzle Content (30% of content)
- **Daily Brain Challenge**: Morning puzzle posts with community engagement
- **Progressive Difficulty Series**: Week-long puzzle progression challenges
- **Puzzle Categories**: Focused content on specific puzzle types
- **Interactive Puzzle Posts**: Facebook-native interactive elements
- **Solution Walkthroughs**: Detailed explanations of puzzle solutions

#### 3. Community Building (20% of content)
- **User Success Stories**: Celebrating community achievements and progress
- **Community Challenges**: Group competitions and collaborative puzzles
- **Discussion Starters**: Engaging questions about puzzle preferences
- **Member Spotlights**: Featuring active community contributors
- **Group Activities**: Organized puzzle events and competitions

#### 4. Behind-the-Scenes (10% of content)
- **Puzzle Creation Process**: How Brain Bender puzzles are developed
- **Team Insights**: Meet the puzzle creators and cognitive experts
- **Platform Updates**: New features and improvements announcements
- **Educational Partnerships**: Collaborations with schools and institutions
- **Company Culture**: Values and mission-driven content

## Facebook Group Strategy

- **Purpose**: Central hub for puzzle enthusiasts and brain training advocates
- **Content**: Daily puzzles, discussions, challenges, and educational resources
- **Moderation**: Active community management with clear guidelines

## Posting Schedule

### Daily Content Distribution
- **8:00 AM EST**: Educational brain training tip or article
- **12:00 PM EST**: Daily puzzle challenge with engagement prompt
- **4:00 PM EST**: Community-focused content or user features
- **7:00 PM EST**: Discussion starter or interactive content

### Content Format Distribution
- **Text Posts** (30%): Educational articles and discussion starters
- **Image Posts** (40%): Visual puzzles and infographics
- **Video Posts** (20%): Tutorial content and puzzle demonstrations
- **Link Posts** (10%): External educational resources and research

## Community Guidelines and Moderation

### Group Rules
1. **Respectful Interaction**: Maintain civil discourse in all discussions
2. **On-Topic Content**: Keep posts related to puzzles and brain training
3. **No Spam**: Avoid promotional content without value
4. **Original Solutions**: Share your own puzzle-solving approaches
5. **Helpful Attitude**: Support other members' learning journeys

### Moderation Protocol
- **Daily Monitoring**: Regular review of posts and comments
- **Quick Response**: Address issues within 2 hours during business hours
- **Warning System**: Progressive discipline for rule violations
- **Content Removal**: Clear guidelines for inappropriate content
- **Ban Policy**: Last resort for repeated violations

## Cross-Platform Integration

### Content Repurposing
- **Instagram to Facebook**: Adapt visual content for Facebook format
- **YouTube to Facebook**: Share video content natively on Facebook
- **Blog to Facebook**: Transform articles into Facebook post series
- **Email to Facebook**: Convert newsletter content for social sharing

### Traffic Direction
- **Facebook to Website**: Drive traffic for detailed content
- **Facebook to App**: Encourage downloads through compelling content
- **Facebook to YouTube**: Promote longer-form educational videos
