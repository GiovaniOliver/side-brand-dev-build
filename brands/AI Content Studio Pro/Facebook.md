## Content Strategy

### Content Mix
1. **Educational Posts** (35%)
   - Tool tutorials
   - How-to guides
   - Tips and tricks
   - Industry insights

2. **Community Engagement** (25%)
   - Questions and polls
   - User spotlights
   - Discussions
   - Challenges

3. **Tool Reviews** (20%)
   - In-depth analysis
   - Comparison posts
   - New releases
   - Updates

4. **Video Content** (20%)
   - Native video uploads
   - Live streams
   - Tutorials
   - Q&A sessions

### Posting Schedule
- **Frequency**: 2-3 times daily
- **Best Times**: 
  - 8-9 AM (morning commute)
  - 12-1 PM (lunch break)
  - 7-9 PM (evening leisure)

### Post Types
- **Link Posts**: Drive traffic to blog/YouTube
- **Native Videos**: Higher engagement
- **Image Posts**: Infographics, tips
- **Live Videos**: Weekly Q&A
- **Stories**: Daily updates

2. **Traffic**: Drive to website/YouTube
3. **Lead Generation**: Email signups
4. **Conversions**: Product sales

**Audience 1: Content Creators**
- Age: 22-45
- Interests: Content creation, YouTube, blogging
- Behaviors: Small business owners, entrepreneurs

**Audience 2: Digital Marketers**
- Age: 25-50
- Job titles: Marketing manager, content strategist
- Interests: Digital marketing, AI, technology

**Audience 3: AI Enthusiasts**
- Age: 20-40
- Interests: Artificial intelligence, ChatGPT, technology
- Behaviors: Early technology adopters
