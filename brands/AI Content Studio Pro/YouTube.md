## Content Strategy

### Video Categories
1. **Tool Tutorials** (40%)
   - Step-by-step guides
   - 10-20 minute videos
   - Screen recordings with commentary
   - Weekly releases

2. **Tool Comparisons** (25%)
   - Head-to-head reviews
   - 15-25 minute videos
   - Side-by-side demonstrations
   - Bi-weekly releases

3. **AI News & Updates** (15%)
   - Industry updates
   - 5-10 minute videos
   - Quick summaries
   - Weekly roundups

4. **Case Studies** (10%)
   - Success stories
   - 10-15 minute videos
   - Real examples
   - Monthly features

5. **Live Streams** (10%)
   - Q&A sessions
   - Tool demonstrations
   - Community events
   - Bi-weekly streams

### Publishing Schedule
