# PAUSEWELL - Instagram Strategy

**Platform:** Primary social media focus
**Year 1 Goal:** 10,000 followers
**Content Frequency:** 1 feed post/day, 3-5 stories/day, 3 reels/week

## Content Pillars

1. **Education (40%)** - Menopause facts, science-backed tips, myth-busting
2. **Community (30%)** - Member stories, Q&As, "you're not alone" posts
3. **Lifestyle (20%)** - Recipes, workouts, self-care, product recommendations
4. **Inspiration (10%)** - Motivational quotes, empowerment, celebrating midlife

## Posting Schedule

- **Daily:** 1 feed post (carousel preferred for engagement)
- **Stories:** 3-5/day (tips, polls, behind-the-scenes)
- **Reels:** Monday, Wednesday, Friday (trending audio + menopause tips)
- **Lives:** Weekly Q&A

## Hashtag Strategy (20-30 per post)

**Primary:** #Menopause #Perimenopause #WomensHealth #HealthyAging
**Secondary:** #MenopauseSupport #HotFlashes #MenopauseJourney
**Branded:** #PAUSEWELL #MenopauseMastery

## Growth Tactics

- Partner with 10-20 micro-influencers (10K-100K followers)
- Monthly giveaways (course access, supplements)
- User-generated content campaigns (#PAUSEWELLJourney)
- Instagram ads: $1,000-2,000/month
- Engagement pods for menopause/women's health
