# PAUSEWELL - Brand Info

## Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** PAUSEWELL
**Tagline:** "Master Your Menopause, Reclaim Your Power"
**Category:** Women's Health & Wellness (Menopause Solutions)
**Founded:** 2025
**Status:** Planning Phase

---

## Brand Concept

PAUSEWELL is a comprehensive menopause wellness ecosystem that empowers women aged 45-60 to thrive through menopause with science-backed solutions, supportive community, and expert guidance. Unlike fragmented or clinical approaches, PAUSEWELL treats menopause as a life stage to master, not a medical problem to endure.

---

## Target Market

### Primary Customer Avatar: "Sarah, The Overwhelmed Achiever"

**Demographics:**
- Age: 48-54 years old
- Income: $75,000-$150,000+ household
- Education: College degree or higher
- Location: Urban/suburban United States
- Occupation: Professional, manager, or entrepreneur

**Psychographics:**
- High achiever, used to being in control
- Values health and wellness
- Willing to invest in quality solutions
- Seeks evidence-based information
- Wants community but values privacy
- Frustrated by lack of good information
- Feels invisible/dismissed by healthcare system

**Pain Points:**
- Hot flashes disrupting work and sleep
- Unexpected weight gain
- Mood swings affecting relationships
- Brain fog impacting work performance
- Feeling "invisible" as a midlife woman
- Confused by conflicting medical advice
- Doesn't want to "just deal with it"
