# PAUSEWELL Membership 12-Month Content Plan
## $39/Month Recurring Revenue Strategy

---

## Executive Summary

This 12-month plan is designed to maximize member retention (6+ months average) through science-backed workshops, active community engagement, and progressive skill-building. Each month builds on the previous, creating a cohesive journey that deepens member commitment and delivers measurable results.

**Key Retention Strategies:**
- Progressive learning path that makes cancellation feel like "losing progress"
- Community accountability systems that create social bonds
- Exclusive resources released monthly (FOMO factor)
- Seasonal alignment with member needs and life stages
- Expert credibility through monthly guest sessions
