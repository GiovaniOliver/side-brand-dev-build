# Startup Brand SOP Template - Sustainable Beauty Box

## Standard Operating Procedures Framework

### 1. Company Formation & Legal Structure
- **Business Entity**: Sustainable Beauty Box LLC
- **EIN Application**: Complete IRS Form SS-4
- **Business Banking**: Green banking (Aspiration/Amalgamated Bank)
- **Legal Requirements**:
  - Operating Agreement
  - Terms of Service
  - Privacy Policy
  - Subscription Terms
  - Return/Refund Policy
  - Environmental Claims Compliance

### 2. Daily Operations

#### Morning Routine (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
1. **Inventory Check** (30 min)
   - Product levels review
   - Expiration date monitoring
   - Reorder notifications
   - Quality control checks

2. **Order Processing** (1.5 hours)
   - New subscription processing
   - Customization reviews
   - Shipping preparations
   - Cancellation handling

3. **Supplier Communication** (1 hour)
   - New product sourcing
   - Sustainability verification
   - Price negotiations
   - Delivery coordination

#### Afternoon Routine (1:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
1. **Content Creation** (2 hours)
   - Blog posts writing
   - Product photography
   - Social media content
   - Educational materials

2. **Customer Service** (1 hour)
   - Email responses
   - Chat support
   - Review responses
   - Feedback processing

3. **Marketing Activities** (1 hour)
   - Campaign management
   - Influencer outreach
   - Partnership development
   - Analytics review

### 3. Product Curation Process

#### Brand Vetting Protocol
1. **Sustainability Verification**
   - Ingredient analysis
   - Packaging assessment
   - Manufacturing practices
   - Carbon footprint review
   - Certifications check

2. **Quality Testing**
   - Product efficacy testing
   - Team testing (2 weeks minimum)
   - Customer panel testing
   - Stability testing

3. **Ethical Verification**
   - Cruelty-free certification
   - Fair trade practices
   - Supply chain audit
   - Worker conditions
   - No greenwashing

4. **Final Selection**
   - Team voting
   - Cost analysis
   - Customer preference data
   - Seasonal relevance
   - Inventory availability

### 4. Box Assembly & Fulfillment

#### Monthly Box Creation
**Week 1**: Product selection finalization
**Week 2**: Inventory receipt and quality check
**Week 3**: Box assembly and customization
**Week 4**: Shipping and tracking

#### Assembly Process
1. Eco-packaging preparation
2. Product placement (heaviest bottom)
3. Educational inserts addition
4. Personalization notes
5. Quality check
6. Sealing with eco-tape
7. Shipping label application

### 5. Subscription Management

#### Customer Lifecycle
1. **Acquisition**
   - Lead capture

   - Onboarding communication

2. **Retention**
   - Monthly customization
   - Loyalty rewards
   - Exclusive offers
   - Community engagement

3. **Win-back**
   - Pause options
   - Cancellation prevention
   - Feedback collection
   - Return incentives

### 6. Inventory Management

#### Stock Control
- **Minimum Levels**: 2 months supply
- **Maximum Levels**: 4 months supply
- **Reorder Points**: At 6-week supply
- **Safety Stock**: 20% buffer

#### Storage Requirements
- Temperature-controlled environment
- Away from direct sunlight
- Organized by expiration date
- Separate areas for categories
- First-in-first-out system

### 7. Quality Control

#### Product Standards
- No expired products (min 6 months shelf life)
- Perfect packaging condition
- Correct labeling
- Seal integrity
- No contamination

#### Box Standards
- All items present
- Correct customization
- Packaging intact
- Inserts included
- Shipping protection adequate

### 8. Customer Service

#### Response Times
- Email: Within 12 hours
- Chat: Within 2 minutes
- Social Media: Within 4 hours
- Phone: Immediate (business hours)

#### Common Issues Resolution
- Missing items: Immediate replacement
- Damaged products: Full refund/replacement
- Allergic reactions: Full refund + consultation
- Subscription changes: Same-day processing
- Billing issues: 24-hour resolution
