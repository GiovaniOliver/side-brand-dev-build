## Brand Overview

### Brand Identity
**Name**: Sustainable Beauty Box
**Tagline**: "Beauty That Gives Back to Earth"
**Domain**: SustainableBeautyBox.com
**Mission**: To curate and deliver eco-conscious beauty products that enhance natural beauty while protecting our planet, making sustainable choices accessible and luxurious.

### Market Position
Positioned at the intersection of the $532 billion beauty industry and the $150 billion sustainable products market, targeting conscious consumers who refuse to compromise between effectiveness and environmental responsibility.
