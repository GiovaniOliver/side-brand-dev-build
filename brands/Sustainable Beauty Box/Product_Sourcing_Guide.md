## Recommended Launch Stack

### Product Sourcing (Months 1-6)
- **Curated products:** Faire marketplace (Net 60, free returns) + Beauty by Earth (10-unit MOQ)
- **Branded hero product:** DR.HC YOURS ($200 setup, 12-unit MOQ) or Blanka (zero MOQ, $29-99/mo)

### Fulfillment
- **ShipBots** or **ShipMonk** for subscription box kitting (both handle small batches 50+)

### Packaging
- **Outer box:** EcoEnclose (100-unit custom MOQ, 100% recycled)
- **Inner tissue:** noissue (low MOQ, FSC certified)
- **If using mailers:** EcoPackables (~$0.21/unit compostable)

### Certifications to Market
- Leaping Bunny (from Indigo Private Label or Vitelle Labs)
- PETA Vegan (from Orglamix)
- EWG-rated ingredients (from Indigo)
- Certified Organic (from Vermont Soap, DR.HC)
