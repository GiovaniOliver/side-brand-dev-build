# Affiliate Monetization Plan - Sustainable Beauty Box

## Revenue Model Overview
Eco-beauty subscription platform monetizing through subscription boxes, sustainable beauty affiliates, marketplace sales, sponsored content, and educational programs targeting $200,000+ first-year revenue.

## Primary Revenue Streams

### 1. Subscription Boxes (50% of Revenue)

**Subscription Tiers**:
**Essential Box** ($25/month)
- 4-5 deluxe samples
- 1 full-size product
- Eco-packaging
- Digital magazine

**Premium Box** ($45/month)
- 5-6 products
- 2-3 full-size items
- Limited editions
- Early access

**Luxe Box** ($75/month)
- 6-8 premium products
- 3-4 full-size items
- Exclusive brands
- VIP perks

**Projected Subscription Revenue**:
- Month 1-3: 100 subscribers = $3,500/month
- Month 4-6: 300 subscribers = $10,500/month
- Month 7-12: 800 subscribers = $28,000/month

### 2. Individual Product Sales (25% of Revenue)

**Marketplace Model**:
- Full-size products from box brands
- 30-40% markup on wholesale
- Member exclusive pricing
- Limited edition bundles

**Product Categories**:
- Skincare: 40% of sales
- Makeup: 30% of sales
- Haircare: 20% of sales
- Body care: 10% of sales

**Projected Marketplace Revenue**:
- Month 1-3: $1,000-2,500
- Month 4-6: $3,000-6,000
- Month 7-12: $8,000-15,000
