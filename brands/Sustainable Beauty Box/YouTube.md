## Video Formats

### Unboxing Structure
1. **Intro** (0-30s): Excitement build-up
2. **Box Reveal** (30s-2min): First impressions
3. **Product Deep Dive** (2-12min): Each item detailed
4. **Value Analysis** (12-14min): Cost breakdown
5. **Sustainability Report** (14-16min): Eco-impact
6. **Outro** (16-18min): CTA and preview

### Review Format
1. **Hook** (0-10s): Results preview
2. **Product Intro** (10-30s): What it claims
3. **Testing Process** (30s-5min): Application/use
4. **Results** (5-8min): Before/after, pros/cons
5. **Sustainability** (8-10min): Eco-credentials
6. **Final Verdict** (10-12min): Recommendations
