# Now Hired US - Facebook Strategy

## Platform Overview
Facebook serves as Now Hired US's community building platform for professional networking, local business connections, and comprehensive career support through groups and educational content.

## Content Strategy

### Facebook Business Page Focus
- **Professional Services**: Comprehensive recruitment and career services presentation
- **Success Stories**: Detailed placement testimonials and career transformation stories
- **Educational Content**: Long-form career advice and hiring best practices articles
- **Industry Insights**: Job market analysis and salary survey reports
- **Community Events**: Career fairs, networking events, and professional workshops

### Facebook Groups Strategy
- **"Career Advancement Network"**: Create supportive community for job seekers
- **"HR Professionals Network"**: Build relationships with hiring managers and HR leaders
- **Local Business Groups**: Participate in metropolitan area business communities
- **Industry-Specific Groups**: Engage in healthcare, technology, and finance recruiting groups

### Posting Schedule
- **Monday**: Career motivation and professional development content
- **Wednesday**: Industry insights and hiring trend analysis
- **Friday**: Success stories and community engagement content

---

**Facebook Goals:**
- Build business page to 5,000+ professional followers
- Create career advancement group with 2,000+ members
- Generate 300+ monthly website visits from Facebook
- Establish local business networking presence in target markets
