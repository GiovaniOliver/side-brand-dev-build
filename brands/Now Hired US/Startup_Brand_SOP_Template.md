# Now Hired US - Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

## 1. Company Formation and Legal Setup

### Business Registration and Licensing
- Register Now Hired US as LLC in Missouri with multi-state operations capability
- Obtain staffing agency licenses for all 50 states (phased rollout approach)
- Establish employment agency bonds and insurance requirements
- Implement EEOC compliance and fair hiring practice protocols

### Intellectual Property Management
- Trademark "Now Hired US" brand name and logo
- Copyright proprietary AI matching algorithms and assessment tools
- Protect client and candidate database systems and methodologies
- Maintain strict confidentiality agreements for all recruitment data

### Regulatory Compliance Tracking
- Ensure compliance with federal and state employment laws
- Maintain GDPR compliance for international candidate data
- Regular audit of ADA compliance in hiring processes
- Background check and verification legal compliance

## 2. Strategic Planning

### Market Position and Service Development
- Position as AI-powered placement specialists with 48-hour candidate submission standard
- Focus on mid-market companies (100-1,000 employees) in professional/white-collar roles
- Develop skills-based hiring expertise and assessment capabilities
- Quarterly market analysis and competitive positioning reviews

### Client Acquisition and Retention Strategy
- Target companies with $78K-$130K average role salaries
- Develop 90-day retention guarantee programs
- Create performance-based pricing models with success bonuses
- Annual client satisfaction surveys and relationship reviews

### Financial and Growth Planning
- Revenue targets: 15-25% of first-year salary for permanent placements
- Temporary staffing markup: 25-60% depending on skill level and urgency
- Monthly financial forecasting and KPI tracking
- Quarterly business expansion and market penetration planning
