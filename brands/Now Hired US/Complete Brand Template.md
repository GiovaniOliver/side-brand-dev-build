# Complete Brand Template - Now Hired US

## 🎯 Brand Overview

- **Industry**: Employment Technology & Career Services

## 📊 Business Model

### Revenue Streams
1. **Employer Subscriptions**: $99-999/month ($500K-2M/year)
2. **Premium Job Seeker Plans**: $19-49/month ($300K-800K/year)
3. **Recruitment Services**: $500-5,000/placement ($1M-3M/year)
4. **Career Coaching**: $97-497/package ($200K-500K/year)
5. **Affiliate Programs**: Resume tools, courses ($100K-300K/year)

### Pricing Strategy
- **Freemium Model**: Basic job search free, premium features paid
- **Tiered Subscriptions**: Individual, Professional, Enterprise
- **Performance-Based**: Pay per successful hire options
- **Value-Added Services**: Resume writing, interview prep
