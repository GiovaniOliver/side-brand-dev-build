# Now Hired US - Twitter Strategy

## Platform Overview
Twitter serves as Now Hired US's real-time professional network for industry commentary, quick hiring tips, and immediate engagement with HR professionals and business leaders.

## Content Strategy

### Core Content Types
- **Industry Commentary**: Real-time reactions to hiring news and job market developments
- **Quick Career Tips**: Bite-sized professional advice and resume optimization tips
- **Job Market Updates**: Breaking news about employment trends and salary changes
- **Success Celebrations**: Brief placement announcements and achievement highlights
- **Professional Networking**: Engaging with HR thought leaders and industry influencers

### Tweet Formats
```
🎯 Hiring Tip: [Quick professional advice in under 200 characters]

Your career success starts with the right opportunities! 

#Hiring #CareerAdvice #ProfessionalDevelopment
```

### Engagement Strategy
- **Industry Hashtags**: #Hiring #Recruitment #HRTech #CareerAdvice #JobSearch
- **Professional Networking**: Engage with HR influencers and business leaders
- **Real-Time Commentary**: Respond to hiring news and industry developments
- **Client Interactions**: Celebrate successful placements and client achievements

---

**Twitter Goals:**
- Build following of 5,000+ HR professionals and business leaders
- Achieve 5%+ engagement rate on professional content
- Generate 200+ monthly profile visits to other platforms
- Establish voice in real-time hiring and recruitment discussions
