# Now Hired US - Instagram Strategy

## Platform Overview
Instagram serves as Now Hired US's visual professional brand builder, showcasing success stories, company culture insights, and career inspiration while building trust with both employers and job seekers.

## Content Strategy

### Core Content Pillars

#### 1. Success Stories and Testimonials (40% of content)
- **Placement Success Stories**: Before/after career transformations with client testimonials
- **Client Testimonials**: Employer satisfaction and hiring success celebrations
- **Career Milestone Celebrations**: New job announcements and professional achievements
- **Company Culture Spotlights**: Behind-the-scenes at client companies
- **Recruiter Success Stories**: Team member achievements and professional development

#### 2. Career Advice and Professional Development (30% of content)
- **Resume Tips**: Visual guides for resume optimization and formatting
- **Interview Preparation**: Quick tips and confidence-building advice
- **Salary Negotiation**: Professional guidance for compensation discussions
- **Career Growth**: Professional development and advancement strategies
- **Industry Insights**: Hiring trends and job market analysis

#### 3. Behind-the-Scenes and Company Culture (20% of content)
- **Recruiter Life**: Day-in-the-life content from recruitment professionals
- **Office Culture**: Team meetings, celebrations, and workplace environment
- **Professional Development**: Training sessions and skill-building activities
- **Community Involvement**: Networking events and industry participation
- **Technology Innovation**: AI matching tools and recruitment technology

#### 4. Industry Education and Market Insights (10% of content)
- **Hiring Trends**: Current job market statistics and forecasts
- **Salary Surveys**: Compensation benchmarking and industry data
- **Skills in Demand**: Most sought-after professional skills and qualifications
- **Company Spotlights**: Featured employers and hiring opportunities
- **Economic Impact**: Job market analysis and business growth indicators

## Community Engagement Strategy

### Professional Network Building
- **Industry Hashtags**: #Hiring #CareerAdvice #ProfessionalDevelopment #JobSearch
- **Location Tags**: Major metropolitan areas and business districts
- **Professional Tags**: Tag featured employers and successful placements
- **Industry Events**: Share conference attendance and networking activities

### User-Generated Content
- **Success Stories**: Encourage placed candidates to share career achievements
- **Company Features**: Highlight client companies and their workplace cultures
- **Professional Testimonials**: Authentic reviews from satisfied clients and candidates
- **Career Journey Posts**: Long-term career development success tracking

## Growth and Engagement Tactics

### Professional Credibility Building
- **Industry Expertise**: Share insights from recruitment professionals and market experts
- **Data-Driven Content**: Post hiring statistics and job market trends
- **Educational Value**: Provide actionable career advice and professional development tips
- **Success Metrics**: Showcase placement success rates and client satisfaction scores

### Partnership and Collaboration
- **Client Company Features**: Highlight employer partners and their hiring successes
- **Professional Services**: Collaborate with career coaches and HR consultants
- **Industry Associations**: Partner with staffing and HR professional organizations
- **Educational Institutions**: Connect with career services and alumni networks

**Instagram Goals:**
- Build professional following of 25,000+ HR professionals and job seekers
- Achieve 5%+ engagement rate on career-focused content
- Generate 500+ monthly website visits from Instagram
- Establish thought leadership in recruitment and career development
- Drive 100+ qualified leads monthly through professional content
