## Target Client Segments

### Primary: Mid-Market Companies (100-1,000 employees)
- **Industries**: Healthcare, technology, finance, manufacturing, professional services
- **Hiring Needs**: Professional/white-collar roles, $78K-$130K salary range
- **Pain Points**: Slow hiring processes, poor candidate quality, high turnover costs
- **Decision Makers**: HR Directors, Hiring Managers, C-Level executives

### Secondary: Growing Small Businesses (50-100 employees)
- **Industries**: Emerging technology, consulting, specialized services
- **Hiring Needs**: Key roles requiring specialized skills and cultural fit
- **Pain Points**: Limited HR resources, competition for talent, hiring inexperience
- **Decision Makers**: Founders, Operations Directors, Department Heads

### Tertiary: Large Corporations (1,000+ employees)
- **Industries**: Fortune 500 companies with specific project needs
- **Hiring Needs**: Temporary staffing, specialized projects, overflow support
- **Pain Points**: Bureaucratic processes, urgent staffing needs, niche skill requirements
- **Decision Makers**: Vendor Management, Strategic Sourcing, HR Business Partners
