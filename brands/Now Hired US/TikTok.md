# Now Hired US - LinkedIn Strategy

## Platform Overview
LinkedIn serves as Now Hired US's primary professional network platform, leveraging the 90% recruiter usage rate to build thought leadership, generate leads, and establish industry authority.

## Content Strategy

### Core Content Types (LinkedIn-Optimized)
- **Industry Insights**: Weekly hiring trend analysis and job market forecasts
- **Success Stories**: Client testimonials and placement achievements with professional metrics
- **Thought Leadership**: Articles on AI-powered recruitment and skills-based hiring
- **Company Updates**: Team growth, awards, and business milestone announcements
- **Educational Content**: Career advice and professional development resources

### Posting Schedule
- **Monday**: Industry trend analysis and market insights
- **Wednesday**: Success stories and client testimonials
- **Friday**: Thought leadership articles and professional advice

### Engagement Strategy
- **Daily Networking**: Connect with 20+ HR professionals and business owners
- **Content Engagement**: Comment thoughtfully on industry leader posts
- **Group Participation**: Active in HR and recruiting professional groups
- **Direct Outreach**: Personalized messages to potential clients and candidates

**LinkedIn Goals:**
- Build network of 10,000+ HR professionals and business leaders
- Generate 50+ qualified client leads monthly
- Establish thought leadership with 5,000+ article views per post
- Achieve 15%+ engagement rate on professional content
