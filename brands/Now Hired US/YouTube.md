# Now Hired US - YouTube Strategy

## Platform Overview
YouTube serves as Now Hired US's educational authority platform for comprehensive career advice, industry insights, and recruitment expertise through professional video content.

## Content Strategy

### Core Video Series

#### 1. "Career Success Masterclass" (Weekly Educational Series)
- **Format**: 10-15 minute in-depth career guidance videos
- **Topics**: Resume optimization, interview mastery, salary negotiation, career transitions
- **Goal**: Establish expertise and provide valuable professional development resources

#### 2. "Hiring Trends Weekly" (Industry Analysis Series)
- **Format**: 8-12 minute market analysis and trend discussion
- **Topics**: Job market insights, salary surveys, skills in demand, industry forecasts
- **Goal**: Position as thought leader in recruitment and hiring intelligence

#### 3. "Success Stories Spotlight" (Monthly Client Features)
- **Format**: 5-8 minute placement success story interviews
- **Topics**: Career transformation journeys, client testimonials, professional achievements
- **Goal**: Build credibility through authentic success documentation

#### 4. "Recruiter Insights" (Behind-the-Scenes Series)
- **Format**: 6-10 minute recruitment process explanation and tips
- **Topics**: How recruiters work, placement processes, industry best practices
- **Goal**: Educate audiences on professional recruitment value and processes

## Monetization Strategy

### YouTube Partner Program
- **Ad Revenue**: Professional content optimized for HR and business audiences
- **Channel Memberships**: Exclusive career resources and early access to content
- **Super Chat**: Live Q&A sessions on career advice and hiring trends

### Lead Generation Integration
- **Career Resources**: Free downloadable guides and templates
- **Consultation Offers**: Career coaching and recruitment service promotions
- **Newsletter Signups**: Professional development and hiring insight subscriptions

**YouTube Goals:**
- Reach 25,000+ subscribers within 18 months
- Generate 100,000+ monthly views across career and hiring content
- Establish authority in recruitment and career development space
- Convert 500+ viewers to qualified leads monthly
