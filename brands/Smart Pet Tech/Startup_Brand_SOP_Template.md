# Startup Brand SOP Template - Smart Pet Tech

## Standard Operating Procedures Framework

### 1. Company Formation & Legal Structure
- **Business Entity**: Smart Pet Tech LLC
- **EIN Application**: Complete IRS Form SS-4
- **Business Banking**: Chase Business or Bank of America
- **Legal Requirements**:
  - Operating Agreement
  - Terms of Service
  - Privacy Policy (GDPR/CCPA compliant)
  - Affiliate Disclosure
  - Pet Safety Disclaimers
  - Data Protection Policies

### 2. Daily Operations

#### Morning Routine (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
1. **Industry Monitoring** (30 min)
   - New product launches
   - Tech news in pet industry
   - Competitor updates
   - Recall alerts
   - Patent filings

2. **Content Creation** (2 hours)
   - Product reviews writing
   - Video scripting
   - Photography/videography
   - Infographic design
   - Tutorial creation

3. **Community Management** (30 min)
   - Forum responses
   - Social media engagement
   - Email replies
   - Comment moderation
   - User support

#### Afternoon Routine (1:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
1. **Product Testing** (2 hours)
   - Device setup and testing
   - Pet interaction observation
   - Data collection
   - Performance metrics
   - Safety evaluation

2. **Partnership Management** (1 hour)
   - Brand communications
   - Affiliate dashboard checks
   - Vet consultations
   - Sponsor relations
   - Collaboration planning

3. **Analytics & Optimization** (1 hour)
   - Traffic analysis
   - Conversion tracking
   - Content performance
   - SEO adjustments
   - Revenue monitoring

### 3. Product Review Process

#### Testing Protocol
1. **Initial Research** (Day 1-2)
   - Manufacturer specs review
   - Competitor comparison
   - User review analysis
   - Safety certification check
   - Price point evaluation

2. **Hands-On Testing** (Day 3-14)
   - Unboxing documentation
   - Setup process recording
   - Daily use with pets
   - Multiple pet testing
   - Various scenario testing

3. **Data Collection** (Throughout)
   - Performance metrics
   - Pet behavior changes
   - App functionality
   - Battery life
   - Durability testing

4. **Veterinary Consultation** (Day 10-14)
   - Health impact assessment
   - Safety verification
   - Professional opinion
   - Recommendation gathering

5. **Content Creation** (Day 15-16)
   - Written review (2,000+ words)
   - Video review (10-15 minutes)
   - Photo gallery
   - Comparison charts
   - Rating assignment

### 4. Pet Testing Guidelines

#### Safety Protocols
- Never force pet interaction
- Monitor for stress signs
- Gradual introduction
- Positive reinforcement only
- Immediate discontinuation if issues

#### Testing Environments
- Home setting (primary)
- Outdoor testing
- Multi-pet households
- Different room setups
- Various weather conditions

#### Documentation Requirements
- Video footage of interactions
- Behavioral notes
- Time logs
- Pet preferences
- Owner observations

#### Article Creation
1. **Research Phase**
   - Technical specifications

2. **Writing Phase**
   - SEO optimization
