## Facebook Group Strategy

### Group Name
"Smart Pet Tech Community - Reviews & Support"

### Group Purpose
A supportive community for pet parents to discuss pet technology, share experiences, get recommendations, and troubleshoot issues together.

### Group Rules
1. Be respectful and kind to all members
2. No spam or excessive self-promotion
3. Stay on topic (pet technology)
4. No medical advice - consult your vet
5. Disclose affiliations if sharing links
6. Search before posting repeated questions
7. No selling without admin approval
