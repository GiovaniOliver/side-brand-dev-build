## Brand Overview

### Brand Identity
**Name**: Smart Pet Tech
**Tagline**: "Where Love Meets Innovation"
**Domain**: SmartPetTech.com
**Mission**: To enhance the bond between pets and their humans through innovative technology that ensures safety, health, and happiness.

### Market Position
The intersection of the $261 billion pet industry and the booming smart home market, targeting tech-savvy pet parents who want the best for their furry family members.
