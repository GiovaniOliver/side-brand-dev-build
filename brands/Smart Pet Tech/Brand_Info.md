To enhance the lives of pets and their owners through innovative smart technology that promotes health, safety, and deeper connections between humans and their furry companions.

To become the leading authority on pet technology, creating a connected ecosystem where every pet owner has access to smart solutions that ensure their pets are healthy, happy, and safe.
