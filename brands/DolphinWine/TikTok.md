# 🐬🍷 DolphinWine - TikTok Strategy
*Making Wine Accessible Through Engaging Short-Form Content*

---

## 🎵 **TIKTOK MARKETING OVERVIEW**

**DolphinWine** uses TikTok to demystify wine culture for younger audiences, making wine education fun and accessible through creative short-form content. Our TikTok strategy focuses on breaking down wine intimidation barriers and attracting the next generation of wine enthusiasts.

### **Platform Objectives**
- 🎯 **Democratize Wine Education**: Make wine knowledge accessible to all ages
- 🏆 **Break Wine Intimidation**: Remove barriers to wine appreciation
- 📚 **Fun Wine Learning**: Educational content that's entertaining and engaging
- 🤝 **Younger Audience**: Connect with Gen Z and Millennial wine newcomers
- 🚀 **Brand Awareness**: Introduce DolphinWine to emerging wine market

---

## 🎯 **TARGET AUDIENCE & CONTENT STRATEGY**

### **Primary Audience Segments**
- **Wine Curious Millennials (40%)**: Ages 25-35, interested but intimidated by wine
- **Gen Z Lifestyle (30%)**: Ages 21-28, discovering adult lifestyle and sophistication
- **Young Professionals (20%)**: Career-focused individuals seeking lifestyle enhancement
- **Content Creators (10%)**: Food and lifestyle TikTokers interested in wine content

### **Content Pillars**
**🎓 Wine Education Made Fun (50%)**
- "Wine 101 in 60 Seconds" educational series
- "Myth-Busting Monday" debunking wine misconceptions
- Quick wine tasting techniques and tips
- "Wine Confidence Hacks" for intimidated beginners

**🎭 Behind-the-Scenes (25%)**
- Sommelier team personality and expertise
- Wine curation process and selection criteria
- Day-in-the-life of wine professionals
- Wine industry insights and trends

**🎉 Wine Lifestyle & Trends (15%)**
- Wine trend participation and adaptation
- Lifestyle integration and casual wine moments
- Seasonal wine content and recommendations
- Wine and food pairing creativity

**🍷 Product & Community (10%)**
- Wine club unboxing and reveals
- Customer transformation stories
- Virtual tasting highlights
- Community wine challenges
