# Complete Brand Template - DolphinWine

## 🎯 Brand Overview

### Brand Identity
- **Name**: DolphinWine
- **Tagline**: "Dive Into Sophistication"
- **Mission**: Create the world's most innovative ocean-inspired wine experience while supporting marine conservation
- **Industry**: Premium Beverages & Marine Conservation
- **Target Market**: Wine enthusiasts, ocean lovers, eco-conscious consumers

### Brand Positioning
- **Primary**: Luxury ocean-themed wine brand
- **Secondary**: Marine conservation advocate
- **Tertiary**: Experiential wine tourism destination

## 📊 Business Model

### Revenue Streams
1. **Wine Sales**: Direct-to-consumer & retail ($2M-5M/year)
2. **Wine Club Subscriptions**: $99-299/month ($500K-1.5M/year)
3. **Experiences & Tours**: Vineyard visits, tastings ($300K-700K/year)
4. **Merchandise**: Branded accessories ($100K-300K/year)
5. **Conservation Partnerships**: Cause marketing ($200K-500K/year)

### Pricing Strategy
- **Premium Positioning**: $45-150 per bottle
- **Limited Editions**: $200-500 collector bottles
- **Club Tiers**: Explorer, Navigator, Captain levels
- **Experience Packages**: $150-1,500 per visit
