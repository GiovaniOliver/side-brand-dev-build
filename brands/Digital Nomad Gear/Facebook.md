# Facebook Strategy - Digital Nomad Gear

## Page Setup
**Page Name**: Digital Nomad Gear  
**Username**: @digitalnomadgear  
**Category**: Product/Service  
**Description**: Curated gear reviews and resources for digital nomads. Real-world tested equipment for remote work and travel.

## Content Strategy

### Content Mix
1. **Product Reviews** (35%)
   - Detailed photo albums
   - Video reviews
   - Pros/cons lists
   - User testimonials

2. **Travel Tips** (25%)
   - Packing guides
   - Destination advice
   - Workspace setups
   - Travel hacks

3. **Community Engagement** (25%)
   - User gear photos
   - Q&A posts
   - Polls and surveys
   - Discussion threads

4. **Deals & Promotions** (15%)
   - Flash sales
   - Exclusive discounts
   - Bundle offers
   - Partner deals

### Posting Schedule
- **Frequency**: 2 times daily
- **Best Times**: 8 AM and 7 PM EST
- **Weekend**: 1 post per day

## Facebook Group Strategy

### Group Name
"Digital Nomad Gear Community"

- Giveaways

## Facebook Ads Strategy

### Campaign Objectives
1. Page likes/follows
2. Website traffic
3. Lead generation
4. Product sales

### Target Audiences
**Audience 1**: Remote Workers
- Age: 25-45
- Interests: Remote work, travel, productivity
- Behaviors: Frequent travelers

**Audience 2**: Travel Enthusiasts
- Age: 22-40
- Interests: Backpacking, minimalism, adventure
- Behaviors: Online shoppers

### Ad Types
- Carousel ads (product showcases)
- Video ads (reviews)
- Collection ads (gear categories)
- Dynamic product ads (retargeting)

### Budget
- **Monthly**: $500-1,000
- **Cost per result goals**:
  - Page likes: $0.50
  - Link clicks: $0.40
  - Conversions: 3:1 ROAS
