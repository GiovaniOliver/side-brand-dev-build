# Digital Nomad Gear - Product Sourcing Guide

## Phase 1: Dropshipping Suppliers

### 1. Spocket (TOP RECOMMENDATION - US/EU Fast Shipping)
- **Website:** [spocket.co](https://www.spocket.co/)
- **Products:** ~1M US and EU products (tech accessories, travel bags, wallets, fitness gear)
- **Shipping:** ~80% ship from US/EU = 2-5 day delivery
- **Plans:** Starter $39.99/mo (25 products), Pro $59.99/mo (250 products), Empire $99.99/mo
- **Private Label:** Available on Pro plan+ (logo on packing slips/invoices)

### 2. CJ Dropshipping (WIDEST CATALOG)
- **Website:** [cjdropshipping.com](https://cjdropshipping.com)
- **Warehouses:** US, China, Europe
- **Products:** Strong in tech accessories, travel organizers, LED travel mirrors, portable chargers
- **Customization:** Private labeling and branding services available
- **Pricing:** No monthly fee for basic access

### 3. AutoDS (BEST AUTOMATION)
- **Website:** [autods.com](https://www.autods.com)
- **Suppliers:** AliExpress, Walmart, Home Depot, Shein, Etsy from one dashboard
- **Features:** Fully automated fulfillment, price/stock monitoring, product importer

### 4. DSers (ALIEXPRESS POWER USERS)
- **Website:** [dsers.com](https://www.dsers.com/)
- **Official AliExpress/Alibaba partnership**
- **Best For:** Bulk ordering, supplier comparison, order automation

### 5. Zendrop (US-FOCUSED)
- **Website:** [zendrop.com](https://www.zendrop.com/)
- **Curated US-focused supplier list**
- **Private label options available**

## Trending Travel Product Categories

- USB travel organizers and cable management cases
- Portable Bluetooth speakers (small form factor)
- LED travel makeup/grooming mirrors (rechargeable, foldable)
- Packing cubes and compression organizers
- Laptop sleeves and tech pouches
- Anti-theft RFID wallets and passport holders
- Portable power banks (10,000mAh+)
- Travel neck pillows with tech integration
- Noise-canceling earbud cases and accessories
- Foldable backpacks and packable day bags

## Recommended Launch Strategy

### Phase 1 - Dropship Launch
- **Primary:** Spocket (US/EU suppliers, 2-5 day shipping, brand experience)
- **Secondary:** CJ Dropshipping (wider catalog for trend testing)
- **Automation:** AutoDS for multi-supplier management
- **Store:** Shopify or WooCommerce

### Phase 2 - Private Label (Year 2)
- **Start with:** PLCM (50-unit MOQ for initial branded product test)
- **Scale to:** Osgoodway (500+ units for proven products)
