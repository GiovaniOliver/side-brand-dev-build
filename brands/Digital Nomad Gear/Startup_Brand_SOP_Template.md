# Startup Brand SOP Template - Digital Nomad Gear

## Standard Operating Procedures Framework

### 1. Company Formation & Legal Structure
- **Business Entity**: Digital Nomad Gear LLC
- **EIN Application**: Complete IRS Form SS-4
- **Business Banking**: Mercury or Wise Business
- **Legal Requirements**:
  - Operating Agreement
  - Terms of Service
  - Privacy Policy
  - Affiliate Disclosure
  - Return Policy
  - International tax considerations

### 2. Daily Operations

#### Morning Routine (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
1. **Market Check** (30 min)
   - New product releases
   - Competitor monitoring
   - Travel trend analysis
   - Community feedback

2. **Content Creation** (2 hours)
   - Product review writing
   - Photo editing
   - Video production
   - Blog post creation

3. **Community Management** (30 min)
   - Discord moderation
   - Email responses
   - Social media engagement
   - Comment replies

#### Afternoon Routine (1:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
1. **Product Testing** (2 hours)
   - Gear evaluation
   - Comparison testing
   - Durability assessments
   - Photo/video capture

2. **Business Development** (1 hour)
   - Affiliate dashboard check
   - Partnership outreach
   - Supplier communication
   - Revenue tracking

3. **Marketing Activities** (1 hour)
   - Social media posting
   - Email campaigns
   - SEO optimization
   - Ad management

### 3. Content Production Pipeline

#### Product Review Process
1. **Research Phase** (Day 1-2)
   - Competitor analysis
   - User feedback collection
   - Technical specifications
   - Price comparisons

2. **Testing Phase** (Day 3-7)
   - Real-world usage
   - Multiple environment testing
   - Durability testing
   - Photo documentation

3. **Content Creation** (Day 8-9)
   - Written review (2,000+ words)
   - Video production
   - Photo editing
   - Comparison charts

4. **Publishing Phase** (Day 10)
   - SEO optimization
   - Multi-platform posting
   - Email notification
   - Social media promotion

### 4. Product Sourcing & Testing

#### Evaluation Criteria
- **Weight & Portability**: Under 2kg preference
- **Durability**: 6+ month testing
- **Functionality**: Multi-use preference
- **Value**: Cost vs benefit analysis
- **Nomad-Specific**: Remote work compatibility

#### Testing Locations
- Coffee shops
- Coworking spaces
- Hotels/hostels
- Airports
- Various climates

### 5. Affiliate Management

#### Program Management
1. **Application Process**
   - Research commission rates
   - Check cookie duration
   - Review terms
   - Submit applications
   - Track approvals

2. **Link Management**
   - Centralized spreadsheet
   - Link cloaking
   - UTM tracking
   - Regular testing
   - Performance monitoring

3. **Revenue Optimization**
   - A/B testing placements
   - Conversion tracking
   - Commission negotiations
   - Promotional calendars

### 6. Dropshipping Operations

#### Supplier Management
- **Primary**: Spocket, Printful
- **Backup**: AliExpress, CJDropshipping
- **Quality Control**: Sample ordering
- **Inventory**: Just-in-time model

#### Order Fulfillment
1. Order received
2. Supplier notification
3. Quality check
4. Tracking provided
5. Customer follow-up

### 7. Email Marketing

- **Monday**: New product alert
- **Wednesday**: Travel tip + gear

- **Sunday**: Destination feature

#### Automation Sequences
1. **Welcome Series** (7 emails)
2. **Abandoned Cart** (3 emails)
3. **Post-Purchase** (5 emails)
4. **Re-engagement** (4 emails)

### 10. Customer Service

#### Response Times
- Email: Within 12 hours
- Social Media: Within 4 hours
- Live Chat: Within 2 minutes
- Discord: Within 1 hour

#### Common Issues
- Product recommendations
- Shipping inquiries
- Return requests
- Technical support

### 11. Growth & Scaling

#### Phase 1 (Months 1-3): Foundation
- 50 product reviews
- 1,000 email subscribers
- $5,000 monthly revenue

#### Phase 2 (Months 4-6): Growth
- 150 total reviews
- 5,000 email subscribers
- $15,000 monthly revenue
- First VA hire

#### Phase 3 (Months 7-12): Scale
- 300+ reviews
- 10,000 email subscribers
- $40,000 monthly revenue
- Team of 3

### 12. Quality Control

#### Content Standards
- Minimum 2,000 words for reviews
- 10+ photos per product
- Video reviews 5-10 minutes
- Honest pros and cons
- Real-world testing minimum 7 days
