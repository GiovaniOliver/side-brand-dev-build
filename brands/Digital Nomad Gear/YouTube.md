## Content Strategy

### Video Categories
1. **Gear Reviews** (40%)

   - 6-month durability updates
   - Comparison videos
   - 10-20 minute videos

2. **Packing Tutorials** (25%)

   - Organization systems
   - Weight optimization
   - 5-15 minute videos

3. **Workspace Tours** (20%)

4. **Destination Guides** (15%)

   - Cost breakdowns
   - Internet speed tests
   - 10-15 minute videos

### Publishing Schedule
- **Monday**: Gear review
