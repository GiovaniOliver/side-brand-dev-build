# Is This Yo BF-GF - TikTok Strategy

## Platform Overview
TikTok serves as Is This Yo BF-GF's primary platform for viral relationship content, dating humor, and connecting with Gen Z and millennial audiences through authentic, relatable dating experiences and modern romance commentary.

## Content Strategy

### Core Content Pillars

#### 1. Dating App Comedy (40% of content)
- **"POV: Your Dating App Experience"**: Relatable dating app scenarios
- **"Red Flag Bingo"**: Spotting dating red flags in real-time
- **"Dating App Profile Reviews"**: Humorous analysis of dating profiles
- **"Swipe Left/Right Reasons"**: Quick dating decision explanations
- **"Dating App Conversations"**: Recreating funny or cringe messages

#### 2. Relationship Reality Checks (30% of content)
- **"Is This Your BF/GF?"**: Questioning relationship behaviors
- **"Relationship Red Flags"**: Educational content about warning signs
- **"Modern Dating Problems"**: Contemporary relationship challenges
- **"Situationship Explanations"**: Defining modern relationship statuses
- **"Dating Standards vs Reality"**: Expectations vs. actual dating experiences

#### 3. Interactive Dating Content (20% of content)
- **"Dating Advice Reactions"**: Responding to dating questions and stories
- **"Rate My Dating Story"**: Community-submitted dating experiences
- **"Dating Trend Commentary"**: Discussing current dating culture trends
- **"Relationship Quiz Time"**: Interactive dating and relationship assessments
- **"Dating Hot Takes"**: Controversial but thoughtful dating opinions

#### 4. Behind-the-Scenes and Personal (10% of content)
- **Personal Dating Stories**: Authentic relationship experiences
- **"Dating Culture Evolution"**: How dating has changed over time
- **"Community Highlights"**: Featuring follower dating stories
- **"Expert Collaborations"**: Dating coaches and relationship experts
- **"Dating Research Insights"**: Interesting dating statistics and studies

## Content Formats and Hooks

### Viral Opening Hooks
- "POV: You're on a dating app and..."
- "Red flag if your partner..."
- "Ladies/Guys, is this your man/woman?"
- "Dating in 2025 be like..."
- "Tell me you're single without telling me..."
- "The way dating apps have us..."

### Video Format Structure (15-60 seconds)
1. **Hook** (0-3s): Attention-grabbing relationship scenario
2. **Setup** (3-8s): Context for the dating situation
3. **Comedy/Insight** (8-45s): Humorous or educational content delivery
4. **Punchline/Takeaway** (45-55s): Memorable conclusion or advice
5. **CTA** (55-60s): Engagement prompt or follow request
