# Is This Yo BF-GF - Instagram Strategy

## Platform Overview
Instagram serves as the visual storytelling hub for relationship content, community building, and lifestyle integration around modern dating culture.

## Content Strategy

### Daily Content Pillars

#### 1. Relationship Memes and Visual Content (35%)
- Dating app screenshot recreations and memes
- Relationship status graphics and quotes
- "Red flags" visual content and infographics
- Modern dating situation illustrations
- Relatable relationship humor posts

#### 2. Dating Advice and Educational Content (30%)
- "Dating tips" carousel posts with actionable advice
- Relationship red flags awareness content
- Communication skills and dating etiquette
- Self-love and confidence building posts
- Dating psychology and insights

#### 3. Community Engagement and UGC (25%)
- User-submitted dating stories in posts/stories
- Community Q&A sessions and advice
- Follower dating experiences and testimonials
- Interactive polls and questions in stories
- Community challenges and dating games

#### 4. Behind-the-Scenes and Personal (10%)
- Personal dating journey and experiences
- Content creation process and insights
- Team and collaboration content
- Industry partnerships and events
- Platform updates and announcements

## Posting Schedule

### Daily Content Distribution
- **9:00 AM EST**: Morning motivation/relationship positivity
- **1:00 PM EST**: Educational content or dating tips
- **5:00 PM EST**: Community engagement or UGC
- **8:00 PM EST**: Entertaining content or memes

### Story Content (3-5 times daily)
- Morning: Daily dating culture question/poll
- Afternoon: Behind-the-scenes content creation
- Evening: Community interaction and responses
- Night: Tomorrow's content preview

## Content Formats

### Feed Posts
- **Single Image Posts**: Relationship quotes and dating memes
- **Carousel Posts**: Multi-slide dating advice and tips
- **Video Posts**: Short dating scenarios and reactions
- **Reel Content**: Trending audio with relationship twist
- **IGTV**: Longer-form dating advice and story content

### Instagram Stories
- **Polls**: "Red flag or green flag?" interactive content
- **Questions**: "Ask me anything" about dating and relationships
- **Quizzes**: Dating personality and compatibility tests
- **Countdowns**: Dating events and content releases
- **User Content**: Resharing community dating stories

## Community Building Strategy

### Engagement Tactics
- **Comment Engagement**: Respond to all comments within 2 hours
- **DM Strategy**: Personal advice and community support
- **Story Interactions**: Daily engagement with follower stories
- **User Features**: Highlight community members and their stories
- **Community Hashtags**: #IsThisYoBFGFCommunity #ModernDatingStories

### User-Generated Content
- **Dating Story Submissions**: Weekly featured dating experiences
- **Red Flag Reports**: Community-submitted warning signs
- **Success Stories**: Celebrating follower relationship wins
- **Dating App Screenshots**: Funny or interesting conversations
- **Transformation Posts**: Dating glow-ups and confidence journeys
