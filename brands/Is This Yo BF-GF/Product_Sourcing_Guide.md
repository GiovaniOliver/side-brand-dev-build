# Is This Yo BF-GF - Product Sourcing Guide

## Print-on-Demand for Relationship-Themed Merch

### 1. Printify (PRIMARY - WIDEST CATALOG)
- **Website:** [printify.com](https://printify.com)
- **Best Products:** Matching couple hoodies/tees, phone cases, mugs, throw pillows, wall art, socks
- **Pricing:** Mugs ~$5, pillows ~$13, socks ~$6, tees from ~$9
- **Target Margins:** 40%+ on all items

### 2. Printful (PREMIUM QUALITY FOR GIFTS)
- **Website:** [printful.com](https://www.printful.com)
- **Best Products:** Embroidered hats, all-over print matching sets, premium canvas prints (relationship quotes/art)
- **Why:** Gift purchases demand higher quality (couples buy merch as gifts for each other)

### 3. Gooten (BEST FOR HOME GOODS)
- **Website:** [gooten.com](https://www.gooten.com)
- **Best Products:** Canvas prints, throw pillows, blankets, tumblers
- **Often cheaper** than Printful on home decor/drinkware

### Marketplace Channels
- **Etsy** - Couples gifts are one of Etsy's strongest categories. Both Printify and Printful integrate directly.
- **Zazzle** - [zazzle.com](https://www.zazzle.com) - Built-in audience for personalized/relationship gifts, 10-15% royalty

### Best Product Categories for This Niche
1. Matching couple hoodies/tees ("His" / "Hers" designs)
2. Couples mugs and drinkware
3. Relationship quote canvas prints and wall art
4. Couples phone cases
5. Throw pillows and blankets
6. Novelty socks sets

### Seasonal Strategy
- **Valentine's Day** - Pre-load designs by January
- **Anniversary** - Year-round evergreen
- **"Situationship" humor** - Trending social content tie-ins

### Recommended Strategy
1. **Shopify + Printify** - Owned brand store, couple-set apparel and novelty gifts
2. **Etsy shop** - Integrated with Printify/Printful for relationship gift search traffic
3. **Zazzle listings** - Passive royalty income on relationship designs
