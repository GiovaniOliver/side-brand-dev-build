# Is This Yo BF-GF - Facebook Strategy

## Platform Overview
Facebook serves as the community building and discussion platform for relationship advice, dating support groups, and longer-form content about modern romance.

## Content Strategy

### Core Content Focus

#### 1. Community Discussion Posts (40%)
- **Relationship Questions**: "What's your biggest dating red flag?"
- **Dating Advice Requests**: Community-driven advice and support
- **Story Sharing**: Anonymous dating experiences and lessons learned
- **Group Discussions**: Facilitating conversations about modern dating
- **Support Threads**: Helping community members through dating challenges

#### 2. Educational Long-Form Content (30%)
- **Dating Psychology Articles**: In-depth relationship insights
- **Research-Based Posts**: Dating studies and statistics
- **Advice Columns**: Detailed responses to dating questions
- **Relationship Guides**: Comprehensive dating and relationship advice
- **Expert Collaborations**: Guest posts from dating coaches and therapists

#### 3. Entertainment and Viral Content (20%)
- **Dating Memes**: Shareable relationship humor
- **Viral Story Reposts**: Popular dating content from other platforms
- **Video Content**: Dating advice and commentary videos
- **Interactive Posts**: Polls and questions about dating preferences
- **Trending Topic Commentary**: Facebook's take on dating culture trends

#### 4. Community Building and Events (10%)
- **Live Sessions**: Dating advice Q&A sessions
- **Community Highlights**: Featuring member success stories
- **Group Events**: Virtual dating support meetups
- **Behind-the-Scenes**: Content creation and brand development
- **Announcements**: Platform updates and new content releases

## Facebook Group Strategy

### "Modern Dating Support" Group
- **Purpose**: Safe space for dating advice and relationship discussions
- **Moderation**: Strict guidelines for respectful, supportive interactions
- **Content**: Daily discussion prompts, advice sharing, success stories
- **Growth**: Targeted promotion and organic community building

### Group Content Calendar
#### Monday - "Monday Motivation"
- Relationship goal setting and self-love content
- Dating confidence boosters and positive affirmations

#### Wednesday - "WTF Wednesday"
- Anonymous dating story sharing and community support
- Advice for handling difficult dating situations

#### Friday - "Feature Friday"
- Community member spotlights and success stories
- Dating wins and relationship milestone celebrations

## Posting Schedule

### Daily Content Distribution
- **9:00 AM EST**: Educational content or relationship advice
- **1:00 PM EST**: Community discussion post or question
- **5:00 PM EST**: Entertainment content or shareable memes
- **7:00 PM EST**: Personal stories or community highlights

## Community Management

### Engagement Protocol
- **Response Time**: Reply to comments within 4 hours
- **Moderation**: Active monitoring for inappropriate content
- **Support**: Providing advice and emotional support to community members
- **Recognition**: Highlighting valuable community contributions
- **Conflict Resolution**: Mediating disagreements with empathy and fairness
