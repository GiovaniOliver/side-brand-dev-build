# Is This Yo BF-GF - YouTube Strategy

## Platform Overview
YouTube serves as the long-form content hub for in-depth relationship advice, dating culture analysis, and educational content about modern romance.

## Content Strategy

### Core Content Series

#### 1. "Dating App Diaries" (Weekly Series)
- **Format**: 15-20 minute episodes reviewing dating app experiences
- **Content**: Real dating app conversations, profile reviews, success/failure stories
- **Goal**: Entertainment and education about online dating reality

#### 2. "Red Flag Academy" (Bi-weekly Educational Series)
- **Format**: 10-15 minute educational videos about relationship warning signs
- **Content**: Psychology behind red flags, how to spot them, when to leave
- **Goal**: Relationship education and safety awareness

#### 3. "Modern Dating Commentary" (Weekly)
- **Format**: 20-30 minute discussion videos about current dating trends
- **Content**: Analysis of dating culture shifts, generational differences, social media impact
- **Goal**: Thought leadership in dating culture space

#### 4. "Community Stories" (Monthly)
- **Format**: 12-18 minute videos featuring subscriber dating stories
- **Content**: Anonymous story readings with commentary and advice
- **Goal**: Community engagement and relatability

## Weekly Content Schedule

### Monday - "Motivation Monday"
- Relationship self-improvement and confidence content
- Dating mindset and personal growth videos
- Success stories and positive dating experiences

### Wednesday - "WTF Wednesday"
- Shocking dating stories and experiences
- Commentary on bizarre dating trends
- Community-submitted wild dating experiences

### Friday - "Feature Friday"
- Educational content about relationships and dating psychology
- Expert interviews with dating coaches and therapists
- Research-based dating advice and insights

### Sunday - "Sunday Stories"
- Community-submitted dating story features
- Personal dating journey updates
- Reflection on dating culture and relationships

## Monetization Strategy

### YouTube Partner Program
- **Ad Revenue**: Optimize for watch time and advertiser-friendly content
- **Channel Memberships**: Exclusive dating advice and community perks
- **Super Chat**: Live stream monetization during Q&A sessions

### External Revenue Integration
- **Course Promotion**: Dating confidence and relationship courses
- **Book Recommendations**: Affiliate marketing for relationship books
- **Brand Partnerships**: Dating apps, lifestyle brands, wellness products
- **Consulting Services**: One-on-one dating coaching and advice
