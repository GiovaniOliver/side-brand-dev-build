## Target Audience Segments

### Primary: Young Adults (Age 18-28)
- **Interests**: Dating apps, social media, relationship content, lifestyle
- **Behavior**: Active daters, social media users, content sharers
- **Pain Points**: Dating app fatigue, relationship confusion, modern dating challenges
- **Marketing Channels**: TikTok, Instagram, Snapchat, dating apps

### Secondary: Millennials (Age 29-35)
- **Interests**: Serious relationships, dating advice, relationship stability
- **Behavior**: Selective dating, career-focused, seeking meaningful connections
- **Pain Points**: Time constraints, dating pool quality, relationship readiness
- **Marketing Channels**: Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, professional networks

### Tertiary: Relationship Content Consumers
- **Interests**: Entertainment, relationship drama, dating culture commentary
- **Behavior**: Content consumers, viral content sharers, community participants
- **Pain Points**: Relationship entertainment needs, social connection
- **Marketing Channels**: All platforms, viral content, community forums

- **Daily Content**: Relationship memes, dating advice, story content
- **Format**: Posts, Stories, Reels, IGTV for longer content

### YouTube (Long-form Content)
- **Weekly Content**: Dating advice series, relationship commentary, Q&A

### Twitter (Real-time Engagement)
- **Daily Content**: Dating culture commentary, trending topic participation

- **Goals**: Thought leadership, viral moments, community building
