# Affiliate Monetization Plan — Is This Yo BF-GF
*Last Updated: March 27, 2026 — COMPLETELY REWRITTEN*

> ⚠️ **CHANGE LOG:** Previous version (2025) described a social surveillance/cheating detection SaaS platform with face matching, social media scanning, and anonymous reporting features. That product model has been **permanently removed** due to serious legal concerns (stalking facilitation, privacy law violations) and ethical issues. Is This Yo BF-GF is an **entertainment and relationship comedy brand only**. No verification tools. No surveillance features. No data collection on third parties.

---

## Brand Identity (Canonical)

**Is This Yo BF-GF** is a relationship comedy and entertainment brand — the humor-first destination for modern dating culture. Think: The Shade Room meets relationship memes meets actual dating advice. We make people laugh at the chaos of modern relationships, then give them useful tools to navigate it better.

**Tagline:** "Navigating Modern Love with Humor"

**Business Model:** Affiliate content site + sponsored entertainment content + digital products

## Primary Revenue Streams

### 1. Affiliate Marketing (40% of Revenue)

#### Dating App Affiliates

**Match.com**
- Commission: $75–$100 per subscription
- Cookie Duration: 30 days
- Why it fits: Premium dating for people ready for real relationships
- Estimated monthly potential: $2,000–$5,000

**eHarmony**
- Commission: $100–$150 per sale
- Cookie Duration: 45 days
- Why it fits: Long-term relationship focus aligns with audience wanting something real
- Estimated monthly potential: $1,500–$4,000

**Tinder/Bumble (via Impact)**
- Commission: 50% first month
- Why it fits: Where the audience is already dating — we add humor to the experience
- Estimated monthly potential: $1,000–$3,000

**Hinge**
- Commission: varies (check affiliate program)
- Why it fits: "Designed to be deleted" brand voice aligns well with our humor angle

**Note:** Ashley Madison has been removed. Promoting infidelity services directly contradicts "Is This Yo BF-GF's" positioning around honest, authentic relationships — even a comedy-forward brand loses credibility here.

#### Relationship & Mental Health Services

**BetterHelp (Couples/Individual Therapy)**
- Commission: $100–$200 per signup
- Cookie Duration: 30 days
- Angle: "When the situationship becomes a full-on crisis, therapy exists."
- Estimated monthly potential: $2,000–$6,000

**Regain (Couples Therapy)**
- Commission: $100–$150 per signup
- Why it fits: Relationship advice naturally leads to therapy referrals
- Estimated monthly potential: $1,500–$4,000

**MasterClass (Relationship/Communication Courses)**
- Commission: $25 per sale
- Why it fits: Content audience is interested in improving, not just venting
- Estimated monthly potential: $500–$1,500

#### Relationship & Self-Help Digital Products

- Relationship communication courses (ClickBank)
- Dating confidence programs
- Self-help / personal development books and courses
- Journaling / mindfulness apps

#### Background Checks (Acceptable Use Only)
Background check services are acceptable when framed around **dating safety** — not surveillance or catching cheaters.

**BeenVerified**
- Commission: $20–$40 per sale
- Angle: "Safe dating 101 — know who you're meeting before the first date."
- Estimated monthly potential: $1,000–$3,000

**TruthFinder**
- Commission: $25–$50 per sale
- Same framing: dating safety, not cheater investigation
- Estimated monthly potential: $1,200–$3,500

**Important:** Do NOT frame background check content as "catch a cheater" tools. Use the dating safety angle only. Surveillance-framed content creates brand risk and potential legal liability.
