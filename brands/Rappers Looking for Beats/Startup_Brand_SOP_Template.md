# Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) Template for a Startup Brand

This document outlines the core SOPs every startup should have in place, regardless of industry. 
Each section can be customized based on your specific business model, team size, and operations.

---

## 1. Company Formation and Legal Setup
- Business registration and licensing
- Trademark and intellectual property management
- Regulatory compliance tracking

## 2. Strategic Planning
- Vision, mission, and value statement documentation
- Business plan and financial projections
- Goal setting and KPI tracking

## 3. Product/Service Development
- Ideation and research process
- Prototyping or MVP development
- Quality assurance and testing

## 4. Branding and Marketing
- Brand identity guidelines (logo, color palette, tone)
- Marketing strategy and channel selection
- Content calendar and social media management

## 5. Sales and Customer Acquisition
- Lead generation and sales funnel process
- Customer relationship management (CRM) setup
- Pricing, proposals, and contract procedures

## 6. Operations and Workflow Management
- Task and project management guidelines
- Supplier and vendor onboarding process
- Standard communication protocols

## 7. Financial Management
- Invoicing, billing, and expense tracking
- Payroll management and tax compliance
- Monthly and quarterly financial reporting

## 8. Human Resources
- Hiring process and job descriptions
- Employee onboarding and training
- Performance reviews and HR compliance

## 9. Technology and Data Security
- IT systems setup and maintenance
- Data privacy and security protocols
- Backup and disaster recovery procedures

## 10. Customer Support
- Support ticket or inquiry handling process
- Escalation procedures
- Feedback collection and satisfaction tracking

## 11. Risk Management
- Risk assessment framework
- Crisis management plan
- Insurance and liability procedures

## 12. Reporting and Continuous Improvement
- Weekly and monthly internal reporting
- Performance review and optimization process
- SOP review and update schedule

---

**Usage:**  
Each section should be expanded into a step-by-step SOP with specific tasks, assigned roles, timelines, and quality standards. 
Customize this template to reflect your startup’s industry, operational complexity, and growth stage.
