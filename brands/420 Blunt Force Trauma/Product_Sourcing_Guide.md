# 420 Blunt Force Trauma - Product Sourcing Guide

## Custom Party Game Manufacturing

### 1. BoardGamesMaker.com (PROTOTYPING / SMALL RUNS)
- **Website:** [boardgamesmaker.com](https://www.boardgamesmaker.com)
- **MOQ:** NONE - order 1 unit
- **Turnaround:** 2-3 days
- **Best For:** Prototypes, testing, initial small batches

### 2. PrintNinja (500+ UNIT RUNS)
- **Website:** [printninja.com/custom-board-game-pricing](https://printninja.com/custom-board-game-pricing/)
- **MOQ:** 500 units
- **Best For:** Card components, game boards, printed materials
- **Free sample packs**, online quote system

### 3. Delano Games (US DOMESTIC)
- **Website:** [delanogames.com/manufacturing](https://delanogames.com/manufacturing/)
- **Location:** USA - Made in America
- **Services:** End-to-end: design through production, assembly, and fulfillment
- **Best For:** US manufacturing, no import logistics

### 4. Alibaba Manufacturers (HIGH VOLUME / LOWEST COST)
- **Website:** [alibaba.com/showroom/board-game-manufacturer](https://www.alibaba.com/showroom/board-game-manufacturer.html)
- **MOQ:** 300-500 sets
- **Pricing:** As low as $0.97/set at scale
- **Best For:** 1,000+ units, lowest per-unit cost

## Recommended Launch Strategy

### Phase 1 - Prototype & Test
- **Manufacturer:** BoardGamesMaker.com (1-10 units for testing)
- **Cost:** Minimal, 2-3 day turnaround

### Phase 2 - First Production Run
- **Manufacturer:** PrintNinja (500 units)
- **Cups/Components:** Snugz USA for branded components
- **Fulfillment:** Flying Cloud Fulfillment

### Phase 3 - Scale
- **Manufacturer:** Alibaba direct (1,000+ units at $1-3/unit)
- **Fulfillment:** Speed Commerce or ShipBob
