"To elevate social gaming experiences by merging the excitement of traditional party games with the growing culture of cannabis, fostering fun, camaraderie, and responsible enjoyment."

"To become the leading innovator in recreational cannabis gaming, transforming social interactions and celebrations through unique and engaging game designs."

## Marketing Strategy

### Social Media Campaign
**Primary Platforms**: Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, YouTube, Facebook, Reddit

**Content Strategy**:
- **Tutorial Videos**: Demonstrate gameplay and rules
- **User-Generated Content**: Encourage fans to share experiences
- **Influencer Partnerships**: Collaborate with cannabis influencers
- **Behind-the-Scenes**: Development process and team insights

### Event Marketing
- **Pop-Up Events**: Cannabis festivals, gaming conventions, 420-friendly venues
- **Launch Party**: Legal cannabis state launch with live demonstrations
- **Tournament Events**: Competitive play events and championships

### Public Relations
- **Press Releases**: Game launch announcements and features
- **Media Outreach**: Cannabis culture magazines, blogs, websites
- **Industry Partnerships**: Collaborations with cannabis brands

## Product Development Strategy

### Prototype Creation
- Cost-effective prototype development using alternative materials
- 3D printing partnerships for startup-friendly pricing
- Beta testing with select group for feedback and refinement

### Manufacturing Plan
- Partner with custom game component manufacturers
- Ensure durable, safe materials for cannabis use
- Scalable production for growing demand

- **Charismatic**: Magnetic personality that draws people in
