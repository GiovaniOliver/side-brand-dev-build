# 420 Blunt Force Trauma Marketing Plan System

A targeted marketing plan to build brand awareness, attract cannabis enthusiasts, and generate pre-orders for our innovative cannabis gaming product.

## Phase 1: Foundation & Cannabis Market Positioning (Weeks 1–2)
**Goal:** Establish clear identity in cannabis gaming niche and ensure compliance.

- [ ] Finalize cannabis-compliant logo and visual branding  
- [ ] Complete mission statement: "Light Up the Party" messaging  
- [ ] Define target personas (cannabis enthusiasts, social gamers, party hosts)  
- [ ] Map cannabis gaming competitors and unique differentiators  
- [ ] Set pre-order pricing and early bird incentive structure  
- [ ] Build age-gated landing page with cannabis compliance

## Phase 2: Cannabis Community Awareness & Lead Generation (Weeks 2–6)
**Goal:** Build awareness within cannabis culture and gaming communities.

- [ ] Create 6-8 cannabis culture content pieces (gameplay videos, 420 content)  
- [ ] Post on cannabis-friendly platforms 5-7x/week (Instagram, TikTok, Reddit)  
- [ ] Engage with cannabis communities and gaming groups  
- [ ] Launch targeted ads on cannabis-friendly platforms  
- [ ] Partner with cannabis influencers for authentic endorsements  
- [ ] Collaborate with head shops and dispensaries for cross-promotion  
- [ ] Create "420 Game Night" lead magnet and email sequence  
- [ ] Build cannabis community email list with game tips and culture content

## Phase 3: Pre-Order Conversion & Cannabis Events (Weeks 4–10)
**Goal:** Convert cannabis community interest into pre-orders and partnerships.

- [ ] Test pre-order offers (early bird, bundle with accessories, limited edition)  
- [ ] Build sales funnel (cannabis content → email nurture → pre-order)  
- [ ] Set up retargeting for website visitors  
- [ ] Launch referral program with cannabis culture rewards  
- [ ] Participate in cannabis events, trade shows, and 420 celebrations  
- [ ] Create tournament event series for community building
