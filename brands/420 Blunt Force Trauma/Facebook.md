# 420 Blunt Force Trauma - Facebook Strategy

## Platform Overview
Facebook serves as our primary community hub and customer service platform, allowing for longer-form content, community building, and direct customer interaction.

## Target Audience on Facebook
- **Primary**: Ages 25-35, cannabis enthusiasts, party hosts
- **Secondary**: Cannabis culture groups, gaming communities
- **Engagement Style**: Community-focused, educational, customer service

## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars (25% each)
1. **Educational Content**: Game rules, cannabis culture education
2. **Community Content**: User-generated content, community highlights
3. **Product Updates**: Behind-the-scenes, development updates
4. **Entertainment**: Memes, cannabis culture humor, gaming content

- **Game Tutorial Videos**: How to play, advanced strategies

## Cannabis Compliance Guidelines

### Content Requirements
- ✅ Age-gated content (21+ only)
- ✅ Responsible use messaging in all cannabis-related posts
- ✅ Comply with Facebook's cannabis advertising policies
- ✅ Include disclaimer: "For legal cannabis use only"
- ✅ No direct cannabis sales or transactions

### Prohibited Content
- ❌ Direct cannabis sales or promotion
- ❌ Content targeting minors
- ❌ Irresponsible use messaging
- ❌ Medical cannabis claims
- ❌ Cross-platform promotion of cannabis sales
