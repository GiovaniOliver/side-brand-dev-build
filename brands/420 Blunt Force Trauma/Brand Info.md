# 420 Blunt Force Trauma - Brand Info

## Overview
**420 Blunt Force Trauma** is a cannabis-themed party game that merges the excitement of traditional beer pong with the growing culture of cannabis. Our unique game design elevates social gaming experiences, fostering fun, camaraderie, and responsible enjoyment among cannabis enthusiasts.

420 Blunt Force Trauma reimagines the classic beer pong game with specially designed cups and gameplay mechanics tailored for cannabis use. The game brings friends together through laughter, competition, and shared cannabis experiences.
