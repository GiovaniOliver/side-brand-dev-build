# 420 Blunt Force Trauma - YouTube Strategy

## Platform Overview
YouTube serves as our primary long-form content platform for comprehensive tutorials, cannabis education, community building, and brand storytelling through detailed video content.

## Target Audience on YouTube
- **Primary**: Ages 21-35, cannabis enthusiasts seeking education and entertainment
- **Secondary**: Gaming communities, party game enthusiasts, cannabis advocates
- **Engagement Style**: Educational, entertaining, community-focused, authentic

## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars (25% each)
1. **Educational Content**: Game tutorials, cannabis education, responsible use
2. **Entertainment**: Gameplay videos, community highlights, behind-the-scenes
3. **Community**: Customer stories, testimonials, community events
4. **Brand Content**: Product development, company culture, industry insights

### Video Types

#### Educational Series
- **Complete Game Tutorial**: Comprehensive how-to-play guide (10-15 minutes)
- **Cannabis Culture Education**: History, advocacy, responsible use (8-12 minutes)
- **Advanced Strategies**: Pro tips and advanced gameplay (5-10 minutes)
- **Setup Guides**: Perfect game night preparation (6-8 minutes)
- **Responsible Use Series**: Cannabis safety and education (10-15 minutes)

#### Entertainment Content
- **Gameplay Sessions**: Full game recordings with commentary (15-30 minutes)
- **Challenge Videos**: Community challenges and competitions (8-15 minutes)
- **Reaction Videos**: First-time player reactions and experiences (10-20 minutes)
- **Community Highlights**: Best customer moments and stories (5-10 minutes)

#### Behind-the-Scenes
- **Development Vlogs**: Product development journey (10-20 minutes)
- **Team Interviews**: Meet the team behind 420 BFT (8-15 minutes)
- **Company Culture**: Day in the life, company values (10-15 minutes)
- **Industry Insights**: Cannabis industry commentary (12-20 minutes)

#### Live Content
- **Game Night Streams**: Live gameplay sessions with community
- **Q&A Sessions**: Community questions and brand interaction
- **Product Launches**: Live launch events and announcements
- **Cannabis Education**: Live educational sessions and discussions

## Cannabis Compliance for YouTube

### Content Guidelines
- ✅ Educational cannabis content focused on culture and responsibility
- ✅ No direct cannabis sales or transactions
- ✅ Age-restricted content (21+) with appropriate warnings
- ✅ Responsible use messaging in all cannabis-related content
- ✅ Compliance with YouTube's cannabis policies

### Video Requirements
- **Age Restrictions**: Apply age restrictions to cannabis-related content
- **Disclaimers**: Clear disclaimers about legal age requirements and responsible use
- **Educational Focus**: Emphasize educational value over promotional content
- **Community Guidelines**: Strict adherence to YouTube community standards
