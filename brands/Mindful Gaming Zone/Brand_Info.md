# Brand Info - Mindful Gaming Zone

## Overview
**Brand Name**: Mindful Gaming Zone  
**Industry**: Gaming Wellness & Mental Health  
**Founded**: 2025  
**Headquarters**: Remote/Digital  
**Website**: MindfulGamingZone.com  

## Mission Statement
To transform gaming into a positive force for mental health and personal growth by providing resources, tools, and community support that help gamers maintain balance, build healthy habits, and enhance their well-being through mindful gaming practices.

## Vision Statement
To create a world where gaming is recognized and utilized as a powerful tool for mental wellness, stress relief, and personal development, while fostering a supportive community that prioritizes health alongside high performance.
