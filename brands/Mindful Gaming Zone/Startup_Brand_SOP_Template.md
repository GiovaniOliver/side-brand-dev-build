# Startup Brand SOP Template - Mindful Gaming Zone

## Standard Operating Procedures Framework

### 1. Company Formation & Legal Structure
- **Business Entity**: Mindful Gaming Zone LLC
- **EIN Application**: Complete IRS Form SS-4
- **Business Banking**: Separate checking account
- **Legal Requirements**:
  - Operating Agreement
  - Terms of Service
  - Privacy Policy
  - Medical Disclaimer
  - Community Guidelines
