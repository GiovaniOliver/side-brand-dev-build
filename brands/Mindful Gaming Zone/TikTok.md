## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars
1. **Quick Wellness Tips** (35%)
   - 15-30 second advice
   - Breathing exercises
   - Posture corrections
   - Eye strain relief
   - Break reminders

2. **Myth Busters** (25%)
   - Gaming addiction myths
   - Mental health misconceptions
   - Research reveals
   - Fact vs fiction
   - 30-45 seconds

3. **Relatable Content** (20%)
   - Gaming struggles
   - Burnout signs
   - Parent perspectives
   - Recovery stories
   - 15-60 seconds

4. **Educational Content** (15%)
   - Mental health education
   - Warning signs
   - Wellness strategies
   - Professional insights
   - 45-60 seconds

5. **Trending Participation** (5%)
   - Mental health trends
   - Gaming trends with wellness twist
   - Challenges adapted
   - Duets and stitches

### Video Formats

**Format 1: The Quick Tip**
```
0-3s: "Gamers, check your posture right now"
4-10s: Demonstration of correct posture
11-15s: "Save this for your next gaming session!"
```

**Format 2: The Myth Buster**
```
0-3s: "Gaming addiction is NOT..."
4-20s: Myth explanation
21-27s: Truth reveal
28-30s: "Follow for more facts"
```

**Format 3: The Transformation**
```
0-5s: "My gaming setup before wellness"
6-20s: Problems shown
21-35s: Changes made
36-45s: Results and benefits
```

**Format 4: The Education**
```
0-5s: Hook with question
6-40s: Educational content
41-55s: Practical application
56-60s: CTA for resources
```
