## Facebook Group Strategy

### Group Name
"Mindful Gamers Community - Wellness & Support"

### Group Purpose
A safe, supportive space for gamers to discuss mental health, share experiences, and find resources for healthy gaming habits.

### Group Rules
1. Respect and kindness always
2. No medical advice - share experiences only
3. Trigger warnings required
4. Confidentiality respected
5. No promotion without permission
6. Crisis resources only from admins
7. Stay on topic

### Group Features
- **Welcome Posts**: New member introductions
- **Daily Check-ins**: Morning wellness checks
- **Weekly Themes**:
  - Motivation Monday
  - Tip Tuesday
  - Wellness Wednesday
  - Throwback Thursday
  - Feature Friday
- **Monthly Challenges**: 30-day wellness goals
- **Expert AMAs**: Mental health professionals

### Moderation Guidelines
- 24/7 monitoring for crisis posts
- Quick response to rule violations
- Supportive intervention approach
- Regular active member rewards
- Clear escalation procedures
