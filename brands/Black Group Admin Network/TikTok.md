# 👥 Black Group Admin Network - TikTok Strategy
*Professional Community Management Education Through Short-Form Video*

---

## 🎵 **TIKTOK MARKETING OVERVIEW**

**Black Group Admin Network** leverages TikTok to reach younger marketing professionals, demonstrate community management expertise through engaging short-form video content, and humanize the professional services industry. Our TikTok strategy focuses on educational content, behind-the-scenes authenticity, and making community management accessible to emerging professionals.

### **Platform Objectives**
- 🎯 **Educate Emerging Professionals**: Teach community management to next generation marketers
- 🏆 **Humanize Professional Services**: Show authentic side of community management work
- 📚 **Simplify Complex Concepts**: Break down community management into digestible content
- 🤝 **Build Young Professional Network**: Connect with Gen Z and Millennial marketers
- 🚀 **Drive Brand Awareness**: Introduce BGAN to younger demographic and future decision makers

### **Video Production Guidelines**

#### **🎥 Technical Specifications**
**Video Quality Standards:**
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920 (9:16 vertical format)
- **Length**: 15-60 seconds optimal, up to 3 minutes for detailed tutorials
- **Audio**: Clear, professional audio with trending music when appropriate
- **Lighting**: Bright, natural lighting for professional appearance
- **Editing**: Clean cuts, text overlays, engaging transitions

**Visual Branding:**
- **Color Consistency**: Brand colors integrated subtly throughout content
- **Text Overlays**: Clear, readable fonts with key information highlighted
- **Logo Integration**: Subtle brand logo placement without being intrusive
- **Professional Appearance**: Team members dressed appropriately for business content
- **Background Consistency**: Clean, professional backgrounds or branded spaces

#### **📝 Content Creation Process**
**Pre-Production:**
- **Content Calendar**: Weekly planning of TikTok content aligned with other platforms
- **Trend Research**: Daily monitoring of trending sounds, effects, and hashtags
- **Script Development**: Key points and call-to-actions for each video
- **Shot List**: Planning specific shots and transitions for more complex videos
- **Props & Setup**: Preparing necessary materials and filming environment

**Production:**
- **Batch Filming**: Filming multiple videos in single sessions for efficiency
- **Multiple Takes**: Recording several versions to choose best performance
- **B-Roll Capture**: Additional footage for editing flexibility and visual interest
- **Audio Quality**: Ensuring clear audio recording throughout filming process
- **Lighting Consistency**: Maintaining professional lighting across all content

**Post-Production:**
- **Quick Editing**: Fast turnaround editing to capitalize on trending topics
- **Text Overlay Addition**: Clear, informative text that adds value to video content
- **Sound Selection**: Choosing appropriate trending or original audio
- **Final Review**: Quality check for brand consistency and professional standards
- **Upload Optimization**: Hashtags, captions, and posting time optimization
