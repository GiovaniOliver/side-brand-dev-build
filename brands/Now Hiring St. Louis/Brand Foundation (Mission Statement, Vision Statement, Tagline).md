# Now Hiring St. Louis - Brand Foundation

## Mission Statement
"To strengthen the St. Louis community by connecting local talent with local employers, fostering economic growth and professional development within our metropolitan area."

## Vision Statement
"To become St. Louis's premier job placement service, known for deep local market knowledge and successful employment matches that benefit both job seekers and employers in our community."

## Brand Tagline
**"Your Local Connection to St. Louis Opportunities"**

## Core Values

### Local Focus
Deep understanding of the St. Louis job market, community dynamics, and regional economic drivers, providing insights that national services cannot match.

### Community
Building a stronger St. Louis through successful employment connections, contributing to local economic growth and community development.

### Relationships
Fostering personal connections with both local employers and job seekers, creating a trusted network within the St. Louis metropolitan area.

### Regional Expertise
Specialized knowledge of St. Louis industries, neighborhoods, commute patterns, and cultural preferences that enhance placement success.

### Economic Growth
Actively contributing to St. Louis area economic development by keeping talent local and supporting business growth through quality recruitment.

## Brand Promise
Now Hiring St. Louis promises to deliver personalized, community-focused job placement services that leverage deep local market knowledge to create meaningful employment connections that strengthen the entire St. Louis metropolitan area.

## Emotional Connection
Now Hiring St. Louis makes clients feel:
- **Connected** to their local community and professional network
- **Understood** through regional expertise and cultural familiarity
- **Supported** by neighbors who care about local success
- **Valued** as contributors to St. Louis economic growth
- **Confident** in local market knowledge and opportunities

## Brand Positioning Statement
"For job seekers and employers in the St. Louis metropolitan area, Now Hiring St. Louis is the local placement service that combines deep community knowledge with personal relationships, unlike national services that lack understanding of local market dynamics and cultural preferences."

## Brand Personality Traits

### Local
- Genuine St. Louis community member and advocate
- Deep neighborhood and regional knowledge
- Understanding of local business culture and preferences

### Personal
- Relationship-focused approach over transactional interactions
- Individual attention and customized service
- Authentic care for community member success

### Expert
- Specialized knowledge of St. Louis job market and industries
- Understanding of regional salary standards and benefits
- Insight into local economic trends and opportunities

### Supportive
- Committed to both individual and community success
- Helpful guidance through local job market navigation
- Advocacy for fair employment practices and opportunities

### Growth-Oriented
- Focus on career advancement within St. Louis market
- Support for local business expansion and hiring needs
- Contribution to regional economic development and opportunity creation
