# Now Hiring St. Louis - Twitter Strategy

## Platform Overview
Twitter serves as Now Hiring St. Louis's real-time local business news and networking platform, engaging with St. Louis Chamber, local businesses, and economic development announcements.

## Content Strategy

### Local Business Engagement
- **Real-Time STL News**: Immediate response to St. Louis business developments and hiring announcements
- **Chamber Event Live-Tweeting**: Coverage of St. Louis Regional Chamber events and networking
- **Local Success Celebrations**: Quick announcements of successful placements and business wins
- **Economic Development Updates**: Sharing St. Louis growth news and expansion announcements
- **Community Support**: Promoting local businesses and economic development initiatives

### St. Louis Professional Networking
- **Business Leader Engagement**: Daily interactions with St. Louis entrepreneurs and executives
- **Local Media Relations**: Engage with St. Louis Business Journal and local business reporters
- **Chamber Connections**: Active participation in St. Louis Chamber Twitter discussions
- **Industry Hashtags**: #STLBusiness #STLChamber #STLJobs #GatewayCityBusiness

### Tweet Formats
```
🏢 St. Louis Business Alert: [Local company news or job opportunity]

Supporting our Gateway City economy, one placement at a time! 

#STLJobs #STLBusiness #YourLocalConnection
```

**Twitter Goals:**
- Build following of 1,500+ St. Louis business community members
- Achieve 8%+ engagement rate on local business content
- Generate real-time leads from St. Louis business news engagement
- Establish voice in St. Louis economic development discussions
