# Complete Brand Template - Now Hiring St. Louis

## 🎯 Brand Overview

### Brand Identity
- **Name**: Now Hiring St. Louis
- **Tagline**: "Your Gateway to the Gateway City"
- **Mission**: Connect St. Louis talent with local opportunities while building a thriving regional economy
- **Industry**: Local Employment & Economic Development
- **Target Market**: St. Louis job seekers and employers

### Brand Positioning
- **Primary**: St. Louis's premier local job platform
- **Secondary**: Regional economic development catalyst
- **Tertiary**: Career resource hub for the Midwest

## 📊 Business Model

### Revenue Streams
1. **Employer Listings**: $99-999/posting ($300K-700K/year)
2. **Premium Memberships**: $19-49/month seekers ($200K-400K/year)
3. **Recruitment Services**: $500-5,000/placement ($400K-800K/year)
4. **Sponsored Content**: Featured employers ($150K-300K/year)
5. **Career Events**: Job fairs, workshops ($100K-200K/year)

### Pricing Strategy
- **Local Business Discount**: 50% off for <50 employees
- **Nonprofit Rate**: Free basic listings
- **Bundle Packages**: Multiple listings discount
- **Government Partnerships**: Special rates
