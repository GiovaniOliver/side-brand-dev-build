# Now Hiring St. Louis - Facebook Strategy

## Platform Overview
Facebook serves as Now Hiring St. Louis's primary community platform, leveraging the strong local networking culture and "Who's Hiring In St. Louis" group engagement patterns.

## Content Strategy

### Core Content Focus

#### 1. Local Job Opportunities (40% of content)
- **St. Louis Company Spotlights**: Weekly features of local employers and their open positions
- **Neighborhood Job Alerts**: Area-specific employment opportunities by ZIP code
- **Industry Focus Posts**: Healthcare, professional services, manufacturing opportunities
- **Local Success Stories**: St. Louis placement celebrations and career achievements
- **Chamber Member Jobs**: Exclusive opportunities from St. Louis business network

#### 2. St. Louis Community Engagement (30% of content)
- **Local Business Support**: Highlighting St. Louis companies and economic development
- **Chamber Event Promotion**: St. Louis Regional Chamber and neighborhood association events
- **Community Involvement**: Charity events, volunteer opportunities, civic engagement
- **Local Economic News**: St. Louis business developments and growth announcements
- **Networking Opportunities**: Professional association meetings and business mixers

#### 3. Career Development for St. Louis Professionals (20% of content)
- **Local Salary Insights**: St. Louis market compensation and benefits trends
- **Professional Development**: Local training, certification, and educational opportunities
- **Interview Preparation**: Tips specific to St. Louis business culture and expectations
- **Career Advancement**: Growth opportunities within the St. Louis job market
- **Relocation Support**: Resources for professionals moving to the St. Louis area

#### 4. Behind-the-Scenes and Company Culture (10% of content)
- **Team Community Involvement**: Participation in St. Louis events and initiatives
- **Local Partnership Highlights**: Collaborations with chambers and organizations
- **Office Culture**: Day-in-the-life content from St. Louis business community
- **Community Impact**: How local placements strengthen the St. Louis economy
- **Personal Stories**: Team members' connections to St. Louis community

## Facebook Groups Strategy

### "St. Louis Career Network" (Primary Group)
- **Purpose**: Central hub for St. Louis job seekers and local employers
- **Content**: Daily local job postings, networking events, salary discussions
- **Moderation**: Community-focused guidelines promoting respectful local networking
- **Growth**: Chamber member promotion and local business networking

### Participation in Existing Groups
- **"Who's Hiring In St. Louis"**: Active participation with valuable local insights
- **St. Louis area neighborhood groups**: Clayton, Chesterfield, Webster Groves communities
- **Professional association groups**: Local HR, healthcare, and business organizations
- **St. Louis Chamber groups**: Regional and neighborhood chamber networking

## Local Community Engagement

### St. Louis Business Network Integration
- **Chamber Event Sharing**: Promote networking events and business mixers
- **Local Business Features**: Highlight successful St. Louis companies and job opportunities
- **Economic Development Support**: Share news about business expansions and job creation
- **Community Partnership**: Collaborate with local organizations on workforce development

### Neighborhood-Level Targeting
- **ZIP Code Specific Content**: Jobs and opportunities by local area preferences
- **Commute-Friendly Posting**: Highlight positions based on transportation and location
- **Local Culture Integration**: Content that reflects St. Louis values and community pride
- **Seasonal Local Events**: Integrate with Cardinals games, festivals, and community celebrations

## Posting Schedule

### Daily Content Distribution
- **8:00 AM CST**: Local job opportunities and company spotlights
- **12:00 PM CST**: Community engagement and networking event promotion
- **4:00 PM CST**: Career development and professional advice
- **6:00 PM CST**: Local business support and economic development news

### Weekly Themes
- **Monday**: "Monday Jobs in the Lou" - week's best local opportunities
- **Tuesday**: "STL Business Spotlight" - featuring local employers
- **Wednesday**: "Workforce Wednesday" - career development and training
- **Thursday**: "Networking Thursday" - events and professional connections
- **Friday**: "Local Success Friday" - celebrating St. Louis placement achievements

**Facebook Goals:**
- Build local business page to 5,000+ St. Louis area followers
- Create St. Louis career group with 2,000+ active local members
- Generate 500+ monthly local job applications
- Establish strong presence in existing St. Louis professional groups
- Drive local community engagement and economic development support
