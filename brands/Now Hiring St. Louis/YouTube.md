# Now Hiring St. Louis - YouTube Strategy

## Platform Overview
YouTube serves as Now Hiring St. Louis's educational authority platform for comprehensive local market insights, St. Louis business features, and career development specific to the Gateway City.

## Content Strategy

### Core Video Series

#### 1. "St. Louis Job Market Weekly" (Weekly Market Analysis)
- **Format**: 8-12 minute local market analysis and employment trends
- **Topics**: St. Louis salary surveys, industry growth, major employer updates
- **Goal**: Establish expertise in local employment market intelligence

#### 2. "Gateway City Employers" (Monthly Business Features)
- **Format**: 10-15 minute company spotlights and workplace culture tours
- **Topics**: Local business success stories, company culture, career opportunities
- **Goal**: Showcase St. Louis business community and available opportunities

#### 3. "Your STL Career Path" (Bi-weekly Career Guidance)
- **Format**: 6-10 minute career advice specific to St. Louis market
- **Topics**: Local networking, salary negotiation, industry transitions
- **Goal**: Provide valuable career development for St. Louis professionals

#### 4. "Chamber Connect" (Monthly Networking Content)
- **Format**: 5-8 minute chamber event coverage and business networking tips
- **Topics**: St. Louis Chamber events, networking strategies, local business connections
- **Goal**: Build community engagement and professional networking

## Local SEO Optimization
- **St. Louis Keywords**: Target "St. Louis jobs," "STL careers," "Gateway City employment"
- **Local Business Integration**: Feature chamber members and local employers
- **Geographic Targeting**: Neighborhood and county-specific content
- **Community Events**: Cover local business awards, networking events, economic development

---

**YouTube Goals:**
- Reach 1,000+ subscribers within 12 months (local market focus)
- Generate 5,000+ monthly views from St. Louis area audiences
- Establish authority in local employment market analysis
- Convert 50+ viewers to qualified local leads monthly
