# Now Hiring St. Louis - Instagram Strategy

## Platform Overview
Instagram serves as Now Hiring St. Louis's visual community brand builder, showcasing local job success stories, St. Louis business culture, and professional opportunities with Gateway City pride.

## Content Strategy

### Core Content Pillars

#### 1. St. Louis Success Stories (35% of content)
- **Local Placement Celebrations**: Before/after career transformations in St. Louis
- **Company Culture Features**: Behind-the-scenes at St. Louis businesses
- **Professional Milestones**: Career achievements within the local community
- **Gateway City Pride**: Success stories that strengthen St. Louis economy
- **Community Impact**: How local employment connects and builds the region

#### 2. St. Louis Business Community (30% of content)
- **Local Employer Spotlights**: Features of St. Louis companies and their cultures
- **Neighborhood Business Districts**: Clayton, Chesterfield, downtown STL highlights
- **Chamber Events**: St. Louis Regional Chamber networking and business events
- **Economic Development**: New businesses, expansions, and job creation news
- **Local Industry Focus**: Healthcare, professional services, manufacturing, technology

#### 3. Professional Development (25% of content)
- **Career Tips**: Advice tailored to St. Louis job market and business culture
- **Local Networking**: Professional association events and community connections
- **Salary Insights**: St. Louis market compensation and benefits trends
- **Skills Development**: Training and certification opportunities in the area
- **Interview Preparation**: Tips specific to St. Louis employer expectations

#### 4. Community and Culture (10% of content)
- **Team Local Involvement**: Staff participation in St. Louis events and charities
- **Gateway City Features**: Local landmarks and community pride elements
- **Seasonal Content**: Cardinals season, local festivals, community celebrations
- **Local Partnerships**: Collaborations with chambers and community organizations
- **Economic Impact**: Celebrating how local employment strengthens St. Louis

## Visual Brand Guidelines

### St. Louis-Themed Photography
- **Gateway Arch Elements**: Incorporate iconic St. Louis imagery in professional content
- **Local Business Settings**: Showcase authentic St. Louis workplace environments
- **Community Events**: Document chamber events and professional networking
- **Neighborhood Features**: Highlight different St. Louis business districts and areas

### Color Palette
- **Gateway Red** (#C8102E): St. Louis Cardinals and local pride representation
- **River Blue** (#0D4F8C): Mississippi River and professional trust elements
- **Forest Green** (#355E3B): Growth, opportunity, and Forest Park references
- **Supporting Neutrals**: Professional grays and whites for business credibility

## Location-Based Content Strategy

### Neighborhood Targeting
- **Clayton Business District**: Professional services and corporate headquarters content
- **Chesterfield/West County**: Healthcare and technology company features
- **Downtown St. Louis**: Urban professional and startup culture content
- **Webster Groves/Kirkwood**: Local business and community-focused content
- **County Areas**: Manufacturing and logistics opportunity highlights

- **#STLJobs #HiringSTL #StLouisCareers #GatewayCityJobs #STLBusiness**
- **#ClaytonJobs #ChesterfieldCareers #DowntownSTL #WebsterGrovesBusiness**
- **#STLChamber #STLNetworking #STLSuccess #YourLocalConnection**

## Community Engagement Tactics

### Local Business Partnerships
- **Chamber Member Features**: Highlight St. Louis Regional Chamber member businesses
- **Cross-Promotion**: Collaborate with local business service providers
- **Event Documentation**: Cover chamber events, networking mixers, business awards
- **Success Celebrations**: Joint posts with local employers celebrating placements

### User-Generated Content
- **Employee Features**: Encourage placed candidates to share workplace photos
- **Business Culture**: Local employers sharing their St. Louis office environments
- **Community Pride**: Team members and clients showing Gateway City love
- **Professional Achievements**: Celebrating career milestones in St. Louis market

## Posting Schedule

### Daily Content Distribution (Central Time)
- **8:00 AM**: Local job opportunity or company spotlight
- **12:00 PM**: Professional development or career tip
- **4:00 PM**: Community engagement or business district feature
- **6:00 PM**: Success story or local achievement celebration

### Weekly Themes
- **Monday**: "Monday in the Lou" - new week job opportunities and motivation
- **Tuesday**: "STL Business Tuesday" - local company and industry spotlights
- **Wednesday**: "Workforce Wednesday" - career development and professional growth
- **Thursday**: "Networking Thursday" - chamber events and professional connections
- **Friday**: "Success Friday" - celebrating local placements and achievements
