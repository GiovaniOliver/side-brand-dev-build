# Now Hiring St. Louis - Brand Info

## Overview
**Now Hiring St. Louis** is a localized job placement service specifically focused on the St. Louis metropolitan area, providing targeted employment solutions that understand the unique job market, industries, and community needs of the region.

## Brand Identity

### Mission Statement
"To strengthen the St. Louis community by connecting local talent with local employers, fostering economic growth and professional development within our metropolitan area."

### Vision Statement
"To become St. Louis's premier job placement service, known for deep local market knowledge and successful employment matches that benefit both job seekers and employers in our community."

### Core Values
- **Local Focus**: Deep understanding of St. Louis job market and community
- **Community**: Building stronger St. Louis through employment success
- **Relationships**: Personal connections with local employers and job seekers
- **Regional Expertise**: Specialized knowledge of local industries and opportunities
- **Economic Growth**: Contributing to St. Louis area economic development

## Target Market

### St. Louis Job Seekers
- **Geographic**: St. Louis City, St. Louis County, surrounding metropolitan area
- **Industries**: Healthcare, manufacturing, technology, financial services, education
- **Demographics**: All skill levels, recent graduates to experienced professionals
- **Situations**: Local job seekers, people relocating to St. Louis area

### St. Louis Employers
- **Local Businesses**: Small to medium-sized St. Louis companies
- **Major Employers**: Large corporations with St. Louis operations
- **Growth Companies**: Expanding businesses needing local talent
- **Industries**: Healthcare (BJC, SSM), aerospace (Boeing), financial services, brewing
