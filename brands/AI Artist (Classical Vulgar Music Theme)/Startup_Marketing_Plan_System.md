# AI Artist Marketing Plan System

A comprehensive marketing plan for launching AI Artist (Classical Vulgar Music Theme) with focus on cultural education, AI innovation, and respectful multicultural music fusion.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation & Cultural Partnerships (Weeks 1–4)
**Goal:** Establish cultural credibility and AI innovation narrative.

- [ ] Develop sophisticated visual brand identity and cultural aesthetic
- [ ] Complete cultural research and community consultation processes
- [ ] Partner with 3-5 cultural music organizations and traditional musicians
- [ ] Create educational content about classical vulgar music fusion concept
- [ ] Build website with AI creation process transparency and cultural education
- [ ] Establish social media presence with cultural sensitivity guidelines
- [ ] Develop cultural advisory board with community leaders and musicians

---

## Phase 2: AI Innovation & Music Release Preparation (Weeks 4–8)
**Goal:** Build awareness around AI music innovation and upcoming releases.

- [ ] Create behind-the-scenes AI music generation content series
- [ ] Produce educational content about multicultural musical traditions
- [ ] Launch "AI Creation Process" video series showing technology innovation
- [ ] Build email list with music industry professionals and cultural enthusiasts
- [ ] Secure features in AI technology and music innovation publications
- [ ] Partner with music tech conferences and cultural festival organizers
- [ ] Create cultural education curriculum for schools and community centers

---

## Phase 3: Music Launch & Cultural Education (Weeks 6–12)

- [ ] Launch debut single "Shimmy on Dat Dick Bitch" with full cultural context

- [ ] Launch virtual listening parties with cultural education components
- [ ] Partner with cultural museums and educational institutions
- [ ] Create interactive cultural music education experiences
- [ ] Build community around cultural appreciation and AI innovation

---

## Phase 4: Community Building & Expansion (Weeks 10–16)
**Goal:** Scale community engagement and expand cultural musical exploration.

- [ ] Release additional singles exploring different cultural traditions
