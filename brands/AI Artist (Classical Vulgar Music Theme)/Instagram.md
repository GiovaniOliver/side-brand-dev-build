# AI Artist - Instagram Strategy

## Platform Overview
Instagram serves as our primary visual storytelling platform for showcasing the sophisticated aesthetic of classical vulgar music, AI creation process, and multicultural artistic fusion.

## Target Audience on Instagram
- **Primary**: Ages 18-35, music enthusiasts, AI technology fans, cultural fusion appreciators
- **Secondary**: Music industry professionals, cultural communities, art lovers
- **Engagement Style**: Sophisticated, educational, culturally respectful, artistically bold

## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars (25% each)
1. **AI Creation Process**: Behind-the-scenes AI music generation
2. **Cultural Education**: Multicultural musical tradition appreciation
3. **Artistic Sophistication**: Classical elegance meets modern boldness
4. **Community Engagement**: Fan-generated content and cultural discussions

### Visual Brand Guidelines
- **Aesthetic**: Classic elegance with modern AI-tech elements
- **Color Palette**: Deep blues, rich golds, cultural accent colors
- **Photography Style**: Sophisticated, artistic, culturally respectful
- **Mood**: Confident, educational, innovative, respectful

### Content Types

#### Feed Posts
- **AI Process Visualization**: Artistic representations of AI music creation
- **Cultural Appreciation**: Beautiful imagery celebrating musical traditions
- **Classic Sophistication**: Frank Sinatra-era inspired aesthetic content
- **Educational Carousels**: Music theory, cultural history, AI technology
- **Professional Photography**: High-quality artistic and cultural imagery

#### Instagram Stories (Daily)
- **AI Creation Live**: Real-time AI music generation process
- **Cultural Moments**: Daily celebration of different musical cultures
- **Behind-the-Scenes**: Studio work, research, cultural consultation
- **Educational Snippets**: Quick cultural and musical education
- **Community Polls**: Engage audience with cultural music preferences

#### Instagram Reels (4-5x/week)
- **AI Generation Timelapses**: Sped-up AI music creation process
- **Cultural Music Samples**: Short clips showcasing cultural influences
- **Before/After**: Original AI composition vs final production
- **Educational Series**: Quick lessons on musical traditions
- **Artistic Expression**: Visual representations of musical fusion

### Posting Schedule
- **Feed Posts**: 1 post per day
- **Stories**: 5-7 stories per day
- **Reels**: 4-5 per week
- **Best Times**: 7-9 PM EST
