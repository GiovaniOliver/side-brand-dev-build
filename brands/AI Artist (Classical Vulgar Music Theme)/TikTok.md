# AI Artist - TikTok Strategy (Detailed)

## Platform Overview
TikTok serves as our creative showcase for AI music generation process, cultural education, and artistic expression through short-form video content.

## Content Strategy
**Primary Focus**: Visual AI creation process, cultural music appreciation, educational entertainment

### Content Types:
1. **AI Generation Timelapses**: Sped-up AI music creation process
2. **Cultural Music Education**: Quick lessons on musical traditions  
3. **Before/After Showcases**: AI composition to final production
4. **Artistic Visualizations**: Visual representations of musical fusion
5. **Behind-the-Scenes**: Studio work and cultural consultation

### Posting Schedule:
- **Frequency**: 1-2 videos per day
- **Best Times**: 6-10 PM EST
- **Content Mix**: 40% AI process, 30% cultural education, 20% artistic, 10% community
