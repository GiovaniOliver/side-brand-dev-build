## 🎯 **MISSION-CRITICAL OPERATIONS**

### **Core Service Delivery Process**

#### **Phase 1: Customer Onboarding & Wine Profile Development (Days 1-7)**

**🍷 Wine Preference Assessment**
1. **Welcome & Taste Profile Setup**
   - Send personalized welcome package with brand story and tasting guide
   - Conduct detailed taste preference survey (sweetness, body, tannins, regions)
   - Schedule optional 30-minute consultation call with certified sommelier
   - Create customer wine profile in CRM with detailed preferences and notes
   - Set initial wine selection parameters based on experience level and budget

2. **Educational Foundation**
   - Provide access to Azure Tide Wine CO University learning portal
   - Share beginner's guide to wine appreciation and tasting techniques
   - Send welcome wine kit with tasting glasses and educational materials
   - Introduce customer to online community and events calendar
   - Set expectations for ongoing education and wine discovery journey

3. **First Wine Selection Process**
   - Sommelier reviews customer profile and selects initial wine recommendations
   - Create detailed tasting notes and pairing suggestions for each selection
   - Prepare personalized wine story cards explaining producer background and region
   - Package wines with premium presentation and educational materials
   - Schedule delivery with optimal timing for wine condition and customer availability

**🎁 Premium Experience Setup**
1. **Delivery Experience Design**
   - Coordinate white-glove delivery service with temperature-controlled transportation
   - Include personalized note from sommelier and detailed tasting guide
   - Provide access to exclusive virtual tasting session for new selections
   - Set up customer account in online portal with order history and preferences
   - Schedule follow-up communication to gather feedback and refine preferences

2. **Community Integration**
   - Invite customer to private Azure Tide Wine CO community groups and events
   - Introduce to local wine events and exclusive member experiences
   - Connect with similar wine enthusiasts in customer's geographic area
   - Provide access to member-only content and exclusive wine releases
   - Set up notifications for events and experiences matching customer interests

#### **Phase 2: Ongoing Curation & Relationship Management (Monthly)**

**🍾 Monthly Wine Curation Process**
1. **Sommelier Selection Process**
   - Review customer feedback and ratings from previous selections
   - Analyze taste evolution and preference changes over time
   - Research new wine discoveries aligned with customer profile
   - Consider seasonal preferences and upcoming holidays or events
   - Select wines that balance education, enjoyment, and discovery

2. **Educational Content Creation**
   - Develop monthly theme connecting all wine selections
   - Create video content explaining wine selections and tasting techniques
   - Prepare written materials about wine regions, producers, and styles
   - Design food pairing suggestions with detailed preparation instructions
   - Develop interactive elements like virtual tastings and Q&A sessions

3. **Premium Packaging & Presentation**
   - Design monthly packaging theme reflecting wine selections and season
   - Include premium presentation materials and tasting accessories
   - Create personalized tasting notes and sommelier recommendations
   - Add exclusive content like producer interviews or vineyard stories
   - Include surprise elements like artisanal food pairings or wine accessories

**📞 Customer Relationship Management**
1. **Regular Check-ins & Feedback**
   - Weekly follow-up after wine delivery to gather immediate feedback
   - Monthly satisfaction surveys with detailed preference refinement
   - Quarterly wine education assessment to track knowledge growth
   - Annual comprehensive review with sommelier to plan upcoming year
   - Special occasion outreach for birthdays, anniversaries, and holidays

2. **Personalized Communication**
   - Customized email content based on wine preferences and education level
   - Personal notes from sommelier team members building individual relationships
   - Exclusive invitations to events and experiences matching customer interests
   - Early access to limited release wines and special collections
   - Tailored gift recommendations for customer's friends and family

#### **Phase 3: Premium Experience & Community Engagement (Ongoing)**

**🎉 Event & Experience Management**
1. **Virtual Experience Coordination**
   - Monthly live virtual tastings with expert sommeliers and guest winemakers
   - Interactive wine education sessions covering regions, styles, and techniques
   - Exclusive access to winemaker interviews and vineyard virtual tours
   - Private group tastings for wine club members and their guests
   - Seasonal celebration events with themed wine selections and activities

2. **In-Person Experience Management**
   - Quarterly local wine events and member meetups in major markets
   - Annual wine country trips and exclusive vineyard experiences
   - Private dining events with wine pairings at premium restaurants
   - Wine education workshops and certification programs
   - Special occasion celebrations and member appreciation events

**🏆 VIP & Loyalty Program Management**
1. **Tier-Based Service Enhancement**
   - Bronze tier: Standard curation with educational materials
   - Silver tier: Enhanced selections with exclusive access and priority booking
   - Gold tier: Rare wine access, private sommelier consultations, and VIP experiences
   - Platinum tier: Bespoke wine services, private events, and concierge-level support
   - Ambassador tier: Industry insider access, producer relationships, and exclusive releases

2. **Recognition & Rewards Program**
   - Monthly member spotlights featuring wine journey stories and achievements
   - Loyalty points system for wine purchases, referrals, and event participation
   - Exclusive merchandise and limited edition wine accessories
   - Birthday and anniversary wine selections with special celebration packages
   - Referral rewards including wine credits and exclusive experience access

### **Wine Storage & Handling Protocols**

#### **🌡️ Storage Environment Standards**
- **Temperature Control**: Maintain 55-60°F (13-15°C) with minimal fluctuation
- **Humidity Management**: 60-70% relative humidity to preserve cork integrity
- **Light Protection**: UV-filtered lighting and dark storage for wine aging
- **Vibration Minimization**: Stable environment without mechanical disturbance
- **Air Quality**: Filtered air circulation to prevent contamination and odors

#### **📦 Packaging & Shipping Standards**
- **Temperature-Controlled Shipping**: Insulated packaging for extreme weather protection
- **Shock Protection**: Foam inserts and protective packaging for wine bottle safety
- **Presentation Quality**: Premium unboxing experience with branded materials
- **Educational Materials**: Include tasting guides and sommelier notes with every shipment
- **Tracking & Communication**: Real-time delivery updates and customer notification system

## 🚨 **CRISIS MANAGEMENT PROTOCOLS**

### **Wine Quality & Delivery Issues**

#### **🍷 Wine Quality Problems**
**Level 1: Individual Wine Quality Issues**
- **Immediate Response**: Replace affected wine within 24 hours
- **Root Cause Analysis**: Investigate storage, handling, and shipping conditions
- **Customer Communication**: Personal apology and explanation from sommelier
- **Compensation**: Full replacement plus additional wine credit for inconvenience
- **Follow-up**: Ensure replacement meets customer satisfaction standards

**Level 2: Batch Wine Quality Problems**
- **Immediate Assessment**: Identify scope of affected customers and wine lots
- **Proactive Communication**: Contact all affected customers before they discover issue
- **Batch Replacement**: Coordinate immediate replacement of entire affected batch
- **Supplier Investigation**: Work with wine producer to identify and correct quality issues
- **Process Review**: Evaluate and improve quality control procedures

#### **📦 Delivery & Logistics Crises**
**Weather-Related Delivery Issues:**
- **Proactive Monitoring**: Track weather conditions and delay shipments when necessary
- **Customer Communication**: Notify customers of delays and provide updated delivery timeline
- **Temperature Protection**: Use enhanced insulation and temperature control for extreme conditions
- **Alternative Solutions**: Offer local pickup or secure delivery locations when needed
- **Quality Assurance**: Inspect all weather-affected shipments for wine condition

**Shipping Carrier Problems:**
- **Multiple Carrier Strategy**: Maintain relationships with backup shipping providers
- **Real-time Tracking**: Monitor all shipments and proactively address delays
- **Direct Communication**: Immediate customer notification of any shipping issues
- **Recovery Solutions**: Express replacement shipping for critical delays
- **Damage Claims**: Streamlined process for handling shipping damage claims

### **Customer Service Escalation Framework**

#### **🎯 Escalation Levels**
**Level 1: Standard Customer Service Issues**
- **Billing questions, delivery preferences, wine selection feedback**
- **Response Time**: Within 2 hours during business hours
- **Resolution Authority**: Customer Experience Manager handles directly
- **Follow-up**: Ensure customer satisfaction within 24 hours

**Level 2: Complex Service Issues**
- **Wine quality complaints, delivery problems, membership cancellations**
- **Response Time**: Within 1 hour during business hours
- **Resolution Authority**: Sommelier and Operations Manager coordination
- **Follow-up**: Personal follow-up call within 48 hours to ensure satisfaction

**Level 3: VIP Customer or Major Issues**
- **High-value customer complaints, public relations issues, legal concerns**
- **Response Time**: Within 30 minutes regardless of time
- **Resolution Authority**: Founder/CEO direct involvement required
- **Follow-up**: Ongoing relationship management and satisfaction monitoring
