# 🐬🍷 Azure Tide Wine CO - YouTube Strategy

## 🎥 **YOUTUBE MARKETING OVERVIEW**

**Azure Tide Wine CO** leverages YouTube as the primary platform for comprehensive wine education, establishing long-form thought leadership, and building an extensive library of wine knowledge. Our YouTube strategy focuses on creating the definitive wine education resource that positions Azure Tide Wine CO as the trusted wine authority.

### **Platform Objectives**
- 🎯 **Wine Education Authority**: Become the premier YouTube channel for wine education
- 📚 **Comprehensive Resource**: Build complete wine knowledge library
- 🏆 **Expert Recognition**: Establish sommelier team as industry authorities
- 💼 **Lead Generation**: Convert viewers into wine club members through education
- 🌐 **SEO Benefits**: Leverage YouTube search for wine education discovery

#### **🎤 "Expert Interviews & Industry Insights"**
- **Winemaker Conversations**: Interviews with renowned producers

### **Video Production Strategy**

#### **🎬 Content Format Structure**
**Educational Videos (15-25 minutes):**
- Introduction and learning objectives
- Comprehensive topic coverage with visual aids
- Practical demonstrations and examples
- Key takeaways and action items
- Call-to-action for wine club or resources

**Wine Tasting Videos (10-15 minutes):**
- Wine background and producer information
- Detailed tasting notes and evaluation
- Food pairing recommendations
- Price point and value assessment
- Purchase recommendations and alternatives
