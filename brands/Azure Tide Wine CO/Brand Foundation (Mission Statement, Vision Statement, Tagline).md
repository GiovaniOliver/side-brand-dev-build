## 🎯 **TARGET AUDIENCE**

### **Primary Customer Segments**

#### **🏙️ Urban Professionals (35%)**
- **Demographics**: Ages 28-45, $75K-$150K income, college-educated professionals
- **Lifestyle**: Career-focused, values quality experiences, entertains frequently
- **Wine Relationship**: Interested in wine but intimidated by complexity, seeks guidance
- **Pain Points**: Limited time for wine education, wants quality without pretension
- **Value Needed**: Curated selection, education, and confidence in wine choices

#### **🎉 Social Entertainers (30%)**
- **Demographics**: Ages 30-50, $100K+ income, active social lives, host frequently
- **Lifestyle**: Loves hosting dinner parties, values premium experiences, image-conscious
- **Wine Relationship**: Appreciates wine as social lubricant, wants to impress guests
- **Pain Points**: Choosing wines for different occasions, creating memorable experiences
- **Value Needed**: Event planning support, impressive wine selections, hosting guidance

#### **📚 Wine Enthusiasts (20%)**
- **Demographics**: Ages 35-55, $125K+ income, established wine appreciation
- **Lifestyle**: Wine-focused travel, collector mindset, values authenticity and quality
- **Wine Relationship**: Knowledgeable but always learning, seeks unique discoveries
- **Pain Points**: Finding unique wines, discovering new regions and producers
- **Value Needed**: Exclusive access, expert curation, educational experiences

#### **💑 Couples & Date Night Market (15%)**
- **Demographics**: Ages 25-40, combined income $100K+, relationship-focused
- **Lifestyle**: Values shared experiences, romantic occasions, milestone celebrations
- **Wine Relationship**: Wine as part of romantic experiences, special occasion focus
- **Pain Points**: Creating romantic moments, choosing wines for special occasions
- **Value Needed**: Romantic experience design, special occasion wines, intimate settings

## 💰 **BUSINESS MODEL**

### **Revenue Streams**
1. **Monthly Wine Club Subscriptions**: Recurring revenue from curated wine deliveries
2. **Individual Wine Sales**: Premium wine sales through e-commerce and events
3. **Experience Services**: Wine education, tastings, and event services
4. **Corporate Services**: Business entertainment, gifts, and team building
5. **Private Consulting**: Personalized wine consultation and collection building
6. **Event Planning**: Wedding and special occasion wine planning services
7. **Partnership Commissions**: Revenue sharing with restaurants and venues
8. **Educational Programs**: Wine education courses and certification programs

### **Pricing Strategy**
- **Premium Positioning**: 20-30% above mass market, 10-15% below ultra-luxury
- **Value-Based Pricing**: Price reflects experience and education value, not just wine cost
- **Membership Benefits**: Subscription members receive 15-20% discount on additional purchases
- **Experience Bundling**: Combined wine and experience packages for higher average order value
