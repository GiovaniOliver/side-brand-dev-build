# Defi Baddies - Instagram Strategy

## Platform Overview
Instagram is the primary platform for fashion-forward crypto content and visual storytelling.

## Content Strategy
- **Feed Posts**: High-quality fashion x finance content
- **Stories**: Daily behind-the-scenes, polls, and quick tips
- **Reels**: Trending audio with DeFi education
- **IGTV**: Longer-form educational content

## Content Pillars
1. **Education (40%)**: DeFi concepts explained stylishly
2. **Lifestyle (30%)**: Fashion, beauty, and crypto lifestyle
3. **Community (20%)**: User features and testimonials
4. **Behind-the-scenes (10%)**: Personal content and process

## Posting Schedule
- **Daily**: 1 feed post and 3-5 stories
- **Reels**: 3-4 per week
- **IGTV**: 1 per week

## Hashtag Strategy
Primary: #DefiBaddies #CryptoFashion #WomenInCrypto
Secondary: #DeFiEducation #CryptoLifestyle #FinancialFreedom
Trending: Research and use 3-5 trending hashtags daily

## Engagement Tactics
- Respond to all comments within 2 hours
- Use interactive stickers in stories
- Collaborate with fashion and crypto influencers
- Create branded hashtag challenges
