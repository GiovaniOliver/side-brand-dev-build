# Defi Baddies - Twitter Strategy

## Platform Overview
Twitter is essential for real-time crypto discussions, news, and thought leadership in the DeFi space.

## Content Strategy
- **Hot Takes**: Bold opinions on crypto trends with fashion perspective
- **Thread Series**: Educational threads about DeFi concepts
- **News Commentary**: React to crypto news with stylish takes
- **Community Engagement**: Participate in crypto Twitter conversations

## Tweet Types
- **Educational Threads**: Step-by-step DeFi guides
- **Quote Tweets**: Add perspective to trending crypto news
- **Original Insights**: Market analysis with fashion analogies
- **Community Content**: Retweet and comment on follower content

## Posting Schedule
- **5-7 tweets per day**
- **1-2 threads per week**
- **Real-time engagement during market volatility**

## Voice & Tone
- Confident and knowledgeable
- Witty and fashion-forward
- Educational but accessible
- Empowering and inclusive

## Engagement Strategy
- Join trending crypto conversations
- Host Twitter Spaces about DeFi topics
- Engage with crypto influencers and founders
- Use polls for community input

## Key Metrics
- Impressions and reach
- Engagement rate
- Follower growth
- Click-through to website/content
