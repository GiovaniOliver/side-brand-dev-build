# Defi Baddies - TikTok Strategy

## Platform Overview
TikTok is perfect for making DeFi education viral and accessible to Gen Z women.

## Content Strategy
- **DeFi 101 Series**: Quick explanations of crypto concepts
- **Fashion Trends**: Crypto-themed outfit challenges
- **Duets**: Respond to finance content with fashion perspective
- **Trending Audio**: Use popular sounds for crypto education

## Content Types
- **Educational**: "POV: Explaining staking to your bestie"
- **Fashion**: "Outfit based on your crypto portfolio"
- **Trending**: Jump on viral audio with DeFi twist
- **Behind-the-scenes**: Day in the life of a crypto baddie

## Posting Schedule
- **Daily posts** (1-2 per day)
- **Consistent timing**: Peak engagement hours
- **Trend awareness**: Quick turnaround on viral content

## Growth Tactics
- Use trending hashtags and sounds
- Collaborate with other creators
- Engage with comments immediately
- Cross-promote from other platforms
