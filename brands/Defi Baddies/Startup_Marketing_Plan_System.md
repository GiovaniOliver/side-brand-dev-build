# Startup Marketing Plan System

A simple, actionable marketing plan to help startups build brand awareness, attract leads, and generate early revenue.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation & Positioning (Weeks 1–2)
**Goal:** Establish a clear identity and market position.

- [ ] Develop logo, color palette, and style guide  
- [ ] Write mission statement and tagline  
- [ ] Identify target audience (3–5 personas)  
- [ ] Map competitors and find differentiators  
- [ ] Set entry-level pricing and offer structure  
- [ ] Build landing page or one-page website  
- [ ] Set up social profiles (2–3 platforms)  
- [ ] Create Google Business Profile (if applicable)  

---

## Phase 2: Awareness & Lead Generation (Weeks 2–6)
**Goal:** Build traffic, interest, and leads.

- [ ] Produce 4–6 cornerstone content pieces (blogs, videos, guides)  
- [ ] Post on social platforms 3–5x/week  
- [ ] Engage with target communities (comments, DMs)  
- [ ] Launch small ad campaign (Facebook/Instagram or Google)  
- [ ] Track conversions and adjust targeting  
- [ ] Partner with 2–3 complementary businesses  
- [ ] Collaborate with micro-influencers or niche newsletters  
- [ ] Create lead magnet (discount, free resource, trial)  
- [ ] Build and launch a 3–4 email nurture sequence  

---

## Phase 3: Conversion & Early Revenue (Weeks 4–10)
**Goal:** Turn awareness into paying customers.

- [ ] Test 2–3 offers (discounts, bundles, free trials)  
- [ ] Build sales funnel (landing page → email nurture → offer)  
- [ ] Automate follow-ups (email + retargeting ads)  
- [ ] Launch a referral program  
- [ ] Set up loyalty discounts or bundle pricing  

---

## Phase 4: Optimization & Scale (Weeks 8–12)
**Goal:** Refine strategy and scale profitable channels.

- [ ] Track KPIs (leads, cost per lead, conversion rate, ROI)  
- [ ] Drop unprofitable channels  
- [ ] Scale high-performing ads and channels  
- [ ] Expand into new channels only if profitable  
- [ ] Automate social, email, and reporting workflows  
- [ ] Build repeatable campaign templates for future growth  

---

## Ongoing Activities
- [ ] Post weekly content (social, blog, or video)  
- [ ] Generate monthly performance reports  
- [ ] Hold quarterly planning sessions to refine and test new strategies
