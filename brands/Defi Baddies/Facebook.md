# Defi Baddies - Facebook Strategy

## Platform Overview
Facebook remains a powerful platform for building communities and reaching a diverse audience interested in finance and lifestyle content.

## Content Strategy
- **Fashion x Finance Reels**: Behind-the-scenes content showing the glamorous side of crypto
- **Educational Posts**: DeFi explanations with stylish visuals
- **Live Sessions**: Weekly "Ask Me Anything" about crypto and fashion
- **Community Building**: Private group for Defi Baddies members

## Posting Schedule
- **Monday**: Market Monday - Weekly market analysis with fashion flair
- **Wednesday**: Wallet Wednesday - Showcase crypto wallets and accessories
- **Friday**: Fashion Friday - Outfit posts with crypto-themed accessories

## Engagement Strategy
- Create polls about favorite DeFi protocols
- Share user-generated content from community
- Host live Q&A sessions about crypto trends
- Cross-promote with Instagram content

## Metrics to Track
- Engagement rate
- Group member growth
- Video view completion rates
- Click-through rates to website
