# Defi Baddies - YouTube Strategy

## Platform Overview
YouTube serves as the hub for long-form educational content and building deeper connections with the audience.

## Content Strategy
- **Weekly Show**: "Defi Baddies Weekly" - market updates and trends
- **Educational Series**: In-depth DeFi protocol explanations
- **Fashion Content**: Crypto-themed styling and shopping
- **Interviews**: Feature successful women in crypto

## Video Types
- **Educational (50%)**: DeFi tutorials and explanations
- **Lifestyle (30%)**: Fashion, daily routines, behind-the-scenes
- **Interviews (15%)**: Guest content with industry experts
- **Vlogs (5%)**: Personal content and experiences

## Posting Schedule
- **Weekly Main Video**: Every Wednesday
- **YouTube Shorts**: 2-3 per week
- **Live Streams**: Monthly Q&A sessions

## SEO Strategy
- Target keywords: "women in crypto", "DeFi explained", "crypto fashion"
- Custom thumbnails with consistent branding
- Detailed descriptions with timestamps
- End screens and cards to promote other videos

## Monetization
- YouTube Partner Program
- Brand sponsorships
- Course/product promotions
- Channel memberships for exclusive content
