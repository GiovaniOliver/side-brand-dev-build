# EcoFlow Living - Brand Info

EcoFlow Living is a sustainable-living and eco-product content brand aimed at environmentally conscious consumers who want practical, affordable ways to live greener.

## Current Product Direction

- Primary model: affiliate-led content commerce
- Core audience: sustainability-minded consumers, especially millennials and Gen Z
- Main value: practical product reviews, lifestyle guides, and implementation advice

## Revenue Direction

- Affiliate product recommendations
- Digital guides and education products
- Sponsored brand placements
- Consultation or curated-service offers
- Later curated bundles or private-label expansion

## Current State

- A brand brief exists in the main dev workspace
- The website exists locally but is not yet operationally mature
- This docs-only repo did not previously include a brand snapshot for EcoFlow Living
