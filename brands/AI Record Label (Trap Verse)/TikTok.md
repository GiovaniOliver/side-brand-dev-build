## Growth Tactics

### Content Hooks (first 1-2 seconds)
- "This AI-generated beat goes crazy..."
- "I made this beat in 30 seconds using AI..."
- "Nobody's talking about this production trick..."
- "This is what a $10,000 beat sounds like vs. AI..."
- "POV: Your producer friend just made the beat of the year..."

### Engagement Boosters
- Ask questions in captions: "Would you rap on this? Comment YES or NO"
- Use "Reply to comments" format for follow-up content
- Pin controversial or high-engagement comments
- Create duet chains with artists
- Run "Best Bar" challenges over TrapVerse beats

### Consistency Rules
- Post at the same times daily for algorithm favor
- Maintain dark aesthetic across all thumbnails
- Use consistent text fonts and colors (white/purple on black)
- Always include audio (TikTok penalizes silent videos)
- Keep captions short and punchy

## TikTok Live Strategy

### Monthly Live Sessions
- **Beat-Making Live:** Create a beat from scratch, let viewers vote on sounds
- **Artist Freestyle:** Signed artists freestyle over beats
- **Q&A Sessions:** Answer production and music industry questions
- **Beat Battles:** Two producers compete live

### Live Best Practices
- Go live during peak hours (7-10 PM EST)
- Promote live sessions 24 hours in advance via Stories
- Engage with every comment during live
- Minimum 30-minute sessions for algorithm boost
