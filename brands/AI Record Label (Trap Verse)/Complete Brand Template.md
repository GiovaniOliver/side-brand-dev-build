## 3. Target Audience

### Persona 1: The Independent Artist
- **Age:** 18-30
- **Profile:** Up-and-coming trap artist, self-produced or looking for beats
- **Pain Points:** Cannot afford studio time, no label support, limited distribution
- **Needs:** Quality beats, mixing/mastering, distribution, promotion
- **Platforms:** Instagram, TikTok, SoundCloud, YouTube

### Persona 2: The Beat Producer
- **Age:** 16-35
- **Profile:** Makes beats at home, sells on BeatStars or YouTube
- **Pain Points:** Creative blocks, slow workflow, competition
- **Needs:** AI tools to speed up production, new sounds, marketplace exposure
- **Platforms:** YouTube, Instagram, Reddit, Discord

### Persona 3: The Hip-Hop Fan
- **Age:** 16-40
- **Profile:** Consumes trap music daily on streaming platforms
- **Pain Points:** Wants to discover new artists, tired of mainstream repetition
- **Needs:** Fresh sounds, behind-the-scenes content, community
- **Platforms:** TikTok, YouTube, Spotify, Twitter

### Persona 4: The Content Creator
- **Age:** 18-35
- **Profile:** Creates videos, streams, or podcasts; needs background music
- **Pain Points:** Copyright strikes, expensive licenses, generic library music
- **Needs:** Affordable, unique trap beats for content use
- **Platforms:** YouTube, TikTok, Twitch
