- "How to Distribute Your Trap Music to All Platforms" (distribution affiliates)

### YouTube Videos
- Beat-making tutorials featuring affiliate products
- Studio equipment reviews and comparisons

- Software walkthroughs and tips

### Email Sequences
- Welcome email with free beat + recommended tools

### Social Media
- Instagram stories with swipe-up links to gear
- TikTok production tips featuring specific tools
- Twitter threads on recommended software

## Affiliate Disclosure

All affiliate relationships are disclosed per FTC guidelines:
- Dedicated `/affiliate-disclosure` page on website
- Inline disclosure on all content containing affiliate links
- Clear labeling: "This post contains affiliate links. We may earn a commission at no extra cost to you."
