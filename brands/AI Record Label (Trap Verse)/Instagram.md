## Reels Strategy

### Top-Performing Formats
1. **Beat Drops** - Dark visual + beat drops at :03 mark, 15-30 seconds
2. **Type Beat Reveals** - "POV: You need a [Artist] type beat" format
3. **Production Process** - Sped-up beat creation in FL Studio/Ableton
4. **AI Demo** - "We let AI make a trap beat" reaction format
5. **Before/After** - Raw beat vs. mixed/mastered comparison
6. **Studio Walkthroughs** - Equipment tours with trending audio

### Reels Best Practices
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds (bass drop, visual glitch)
- Use trending audio when relevant to music niche
- Text overlays for context (works with sound off)
- End with CTA: "Save this beat" / "Follow for more" / "Link in bio"
- Post at peak times: 11 AM, 2 PM, 7 PM EST
