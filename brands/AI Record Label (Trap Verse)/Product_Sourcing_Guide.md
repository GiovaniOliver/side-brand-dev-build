# Trap Verse (AI Record Label) - Product Sourcing Guide

## Print-on-Demand for Streetwear Merch

### 1. Fourthwall (RECOMMENDED STOREFRONT FOR MUSICIANS)
- **Website:** [fourthwall.com](https://fourthwall.com)
- **No monthly fees**, keep 100% of physical product profits
- **Integrations:** YouTube Merch Shelf, Twitch, TikTok Shop, Spotify, Discord
- **Best For:** Artist-native storefront with music platform integrations

### 2. Printify (BEST MARGINS)
- **Website:** [printify.com](https://printify.com)
- **Products:** 1,300+ items, Bella+Canvas tees from ~$9.11, hoodies from ~$15-20
- **Plans:** Free or Premium $24.99/mo (20% off all products)
- **Integrations:** Shopify, WooCommerce, Etsy, Wix, Squarespace

### 3. Printful (BEST QUALITY FOR PREMIUM ITEMS)
- **Website:** [printful.com/band-merchandise](https://www.printful.com/band-merchandise)
- **Products:** Hoodies ~$14.94, hats ~$18.94
- **Bulk Discounts:** Up to 55% off at 25+ units
- **Best For:** Embroidered hats, premium items, all-over prints
- **Integrations:** Shopify, Spotify Merch Hub, YouTube Merch Shelf

### 4. Gooten
- **Website:** [gooten.com](https://www.gooten.com)
- **Products:** 525+ items, 70+ fulfillment locations
- **Pricing:** 10-40% cheaper than Printful on standard items
- **No monthly fees**

### Recommended Strategy
- **Storefront:** Fourthwall (YouTube + Spotify integration)
- **Fulfillment Backend:** Printify for hoodies/tees (best margins)
- **Premium Items:** Printful for embroidered hats and all-over prints

## Vinyl Pressing Services

### 1. Disc Makers (MOST AFFORDABLE)
- **Website:** [discmakers.com/products/vinyl](https://www.discmakers.com/products/vinyl)
- **100 units** (12" LP, black vinyl, color jacket): ~$999 + ~$150 freight = ~$11.69/unit
- **200 units:** ~$8.80/unit
- **300 units:** ~$7.60/unit
- **Turnaround:** 4-6 weeks
- **Includes:** Mastering, cutting, pressing, jackets, labels, shrinkwrap

### 2. Memphis Record Pressing
- **Website:** [memphisrecordpressing.com](https://memphisrecordpressing.com)
- **Short-Run:** 100, 200, or 300 units
- **Includes:** 140g vinyl (black or EcoMix), full-color center labels, jackets, shrinkwrap
- **Turnaround:** 4-6 weeks

### 3. Solid Merch (LIMITED EDITION SPECIALIST)
- **Website:** [solid-merch.com/vinyl-pressing](https://www.solid-merch.com/vinyl-pressing/)
- **MOQ:** 100 units (preset), 250 units (full custom)
- **Custom Options:** Splatter, color-in-color, embossed jackets, foil printing, hand-numbered
- **Best For:** Collector limited editions at premium pricing ($35-50/unit retail)

### 4. Gotta Groove Records
- **Website:** [gottagrooverecords.com/pricing-online-ordering](https://www.gottagrooverecords.com/pricing-online-ordering/)
- **Transparent online pricing**
- **Location:** Cleveland, OH

### Vinyl Strategy
- **First Run:** Disc Makers (100 units at ~$999 to test demand)
- **Collector Editions:** Solid Merch (custom colorways for premium retail pricing)
