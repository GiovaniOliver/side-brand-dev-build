## Content Strategy

### Content Mix
1. **Music Releases** (30%) - Singles, videos, announcements
2. **Live Videos** (20%) - Performances, freestyles, talks
3. **Community Posts** (20%) - Hood stories, giveback
4. **Behind The Scenes** (15%) - Studio, tour, life
5. **Fan Engagement** (15%) - Contests, features, shoutouts

### Posting Schedule
- **Frequency**: 1-2 posts daily
- **Peak Times**:
  - 9 AM EST (morning motivation)
  - 3 PM EST (afternoon engagement)
  - 8 PM EST (prime time)
