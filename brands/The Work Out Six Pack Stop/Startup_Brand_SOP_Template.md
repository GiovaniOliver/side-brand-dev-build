# The Work Out Six Pack Stop - Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

## 1. Company Formation and Fitness Business Setup

### Business Registration and Licensing
- Register The Work Out Six Pack Stop as LLC in Missouri
- Obtain necessary fitness business licenses and insurance coverage
- Establish liability insurance for fitness instruction and online training
- Trademark brand name, logo, and signature program names

### Health and Safety Compliance
- Maintain current CPR and first aid certifications for all trainers
- Implement safety guidelines for all workout programming and instruction
- Establish medical clearance requirements for high-intensity programs
- Create injury prevention protocols and emergency response procedures

### Professional Certifications and Standards
- Maintain NASM, ACSM, or equivalent personal training certifications
- Pursue continuing education in core training and exercise science
- Stay current with fitness industry best practices and safety standards
- Regular skill development in video production and online coaching

## 2. Fitness Program Development and Quality Standards

### Workout Program Creation
- Evidence-based exercise selection with scientific backing for core development
- Progressive difficulty levels from beginner to advanced training
- Proper form demonstration with clear instruction and safety cues
- Program testing with beta clients before public launch

### Quality Assurance Protocols
- Video production standards for workout content: HD quality, clear audio, proper lighting
- Exercise demonstration review by certified fitness professionals
- Program effectiveness testing with measurable results tracking
- Regular content updates based on client feedback and results

### Safety and Modification Standards
- Alternative exercises for different fitness levels and physical limitations
- Clear contraindication warnings for high-risk populations
- Injury prevention education and warm-up/cool-down protocols
- Medical professional consultation for specialized populations

## 3. Content Creation and Digital Marketing

### Video Production Workflow
- Weekly content planning with workout themes and educational topics
- Professional filming setup with proper lighting, audio, and camera angles
- Post-production editing with graphics, music, and instructional overlays
- Content scheduling across multiple platforms with platform-specific optimization

### Social Media Management
- Daily posting schedule across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, Twitter
- Community engagement and response to comments within 2 hours
- User-generated content collection and feature campaigns
- Influencer outreach and collaboration partnership development

### Educational Content Development
- Monthly blog articles on fitness science, nutrition, and training methodology
- Email newsletter with workout tips, motivation, and program updates
- Free resource creation: workout guides, nutrition tips, progress tracking tools
- Webinar and live session planning for community education and engagement
