## Segmentation Strategy

### Subscriber Segments
1. **New Subscribers** (0-30 days): Welcome series focus
2. **Engaged Prospects** (31-90 days): Educational content and soft sales
3. **Program Customers**: Onboarding and retention content
4. **Past Customers**: Re-engagement and advanced programs
5. **VIP Members**: Exclusive content and early access

### Behavioral Triggers
- **High Engagement**: Advanced content and program offers
- **Low Engagement**: Re-engagement campaigns
- **Purchase Behavior**: Upsell and cross-sell opportunities
- **Support Requests**: Additional help and resources
- **Referral Activity**: Reward and recognition programs
