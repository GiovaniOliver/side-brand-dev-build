## Target Audience Segments

### Primary: Aesthetic Achievers (Age 22-32)
- **Goals**: Visible abs, aesthetic physique, social media worthy transformations
- **Spending**: $200-$500 annually on fitness programs and supplements
- **Motivation**: Looking good, confidence, social recognition
- **Pain Points**: Lack of visible results, time constraints, unclear programming

### Secondary: Busy Professionals (Age 28-42)
- **Goals**: Health improvement, stress relief, functional strength
- **Spending**: $300-$800 annually on fitness and wellness
- **Motivation**: Health, energy, work-life balance, longevity
- **Pain Points**: Time limitations, gym access, motivation consistency

### Tertiary: Fitness Enthusiasts (Age 25-40)
- **Goals**: Advanced training, performance improvement, new challenges
- **Spending**: $500-$1,200 annually on fitness programs and equipment
- **Motivation**: Performance, knowledge, community, progression
- **Pain Points**: Plateaus, advanced programming needs, specialization gaps
