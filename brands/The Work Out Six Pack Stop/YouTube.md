1. **Intro** (0-30s): What you'll achieve

4. **Cool-down** (15-17min): Stretching
5. **Outro** (17-20min): Tips and CTA

### Educational Format
1. **Hook** (0-10s): Problem identification
2. **Introduction** (10-30s): What you'll learn
3. **Main Content** (30s-8min): Deep dive
4. **Demonstration** (8-10min): Practical application
5. **Summary** (10-12min): Key takeaways
