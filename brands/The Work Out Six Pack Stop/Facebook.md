1. **Workout Videos** (30%) - Full routines, quick circuits
2. **Educational Posts** (25%) - Nutrition, form tips, science
3. **Transformations** (20%) - Success stories, progress
4. **Community Engagement** (15%) - Challenges, polls, Q&A
5. **Promotional** (10%) - Programs, offers, events

- **Frequency**: 2-3 posts daily
- **Best Times**: 5 AM, 12 PM, 7 PM EST
- **Live Workouts**: Saturdays 9 AM EST
