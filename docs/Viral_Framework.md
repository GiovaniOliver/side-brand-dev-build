
# Viral Framework - Markdown Format

| Level | Title                              | Goal                                                                                      | Team                                                                                               | Customer                                                                                           | Business Model                                                                                     | Investability                                                                                         |
|-------|------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1     | Establishing the Founding Team     | You’ve identified a problem worth solving.                                               | At least two team members with complementary skills.                                                | Understand the problem through direct experience.                                                 | Identified a clear problem.                                                                          | Clear problem, relevant team.                                                                          |
| 2     | Setting the Vision                 | You can clearly articulate the opportunity you’re pursuing.                              | Experience with target customer or problem.                                                         | Deep understanding of target customer.                                                            | Clear value proposition.                                                                             | Vision for the future.                                                                                 |
| 3     | Solidifying the Value Proposition  | You’ve built a product that solves a real problem.                                       | Ability to build and sell the product.                                                              | Customers want what you're building.                                                              | Functional prototype exists.                                                                         | Early believers in the product.                                                                        |
| 4     | Validating an Investable Market    | You have early signs that you’re in a big, growing market.                               | Connected to experts in your industry.                                                              | Industry needs this solution.                                                                     | Market demand is validated.                                                                          | Large, growing market.                                                                                 |
| 5     | Proving a Profitable Business Model| You’ve figured out how to make money and prove demand.                                   | Team understands costs, pricing, and revenue model.                                                 | Customers are willing to pay.                                                                     | Sales pipeline exists.                                                                               | Clear path to profitability.                                                                           |
| 6     | Moving Beyond Early Adopters       | You’re growing past your early users and proving scale.                                  | Team is executing and adapting quickly.                                                             | Users are returning and referring others.                                                         | Repeatable and scalable model.                                                                       | Demonstrated traction.                                                                                 |
| 7     | Hitting Product-Market Fit         | You’ve nailed a solution people want, and know how to sell it.                           | Team is growing and hiring for scale.                                                               | Customer acquisition cost is low, lifetime value is high.                                        | Predictable growth engine.                                                                           | Strong product-market fit.                                                                             |
| 8     | Scaling Up                         | You’re a market leader with significant traction.                                         | Company is hiring and building culture.                                                             | Strong brand affinity.                                                                            | Business model supports growth.                                                                      | Market leadership position.                                                                            |
| 9     | Exit in Sight                      | You're an attractive acquisition or IPO candidate.                                       | Leadership team built for scale and sustainability.                                                 | Customer and community advocacy.                                                                 | Business model is proven and defensible.                                                             | Clear acquisition or IPO path.                                                                         |
