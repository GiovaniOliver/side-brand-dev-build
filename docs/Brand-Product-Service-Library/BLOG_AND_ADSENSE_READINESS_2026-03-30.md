# Blog And AdSense Readiness

**Date:** March 30, 2026
**Scope:** Current local website folders and seeded blog directories.

## Core Rule

Blog content is a traffic and trust layer across the portfolio. Google AdSense should be treated as a secondary monetization layer after:

1. Primary offer CTAs
2. Email capture
3. Affiliate or partner offers
4. Owned subscriptions, services, or product sales

## Missing Blog Operations Across The Portfolio

1. Every brand needs a defined CTA map:
   homepage CTA, blog CTA, sidebar CTA, and exit CTA.
2. Every brand needs one lead magnet or low-friction capture offer.
3. Category pages need to be tied to revenue themes:
   product reviews, tutorials, comparisons, case studies, and buyer guides.
4. High-AdSense brands need a split between:
   informational posts for ad inventory and commercial-intent posts for conversion.
5. Low-AdSense brands should push readers toward:
   bookings, memberships, services, subscriptions, or product pages instead.

## Required Blog Workflow Per Brand

Each brand blog should map content into one of four roles:

1. **Traffic**
   Rank for search and reach new users.
2. **Trust**
   Educate the reader and establish authority.
3. **Transition**
   Move the reader into email capture or a product page.
4. **Transaction**
   Support a direct purchase, booking, signup, or partner click.
