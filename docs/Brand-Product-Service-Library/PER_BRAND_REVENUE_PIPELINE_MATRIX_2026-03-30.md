## Decision Rule For The Next 90 Days

Use this matrix to decide what to build first:

1. If a brand has a clear first offer, seeded content, and a realistic partner stack, move it into implementation.
2. If a brand has traffic potential but no offer ladder, lock the product stack before adding more content.
3. If a brand has neither a clear offer nor a clear partner stack, stop adding complexity and finish the strategy first.
